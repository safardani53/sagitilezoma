Star Wars has been inspiring awesome Halloween costumes for decades. But this year it’s not Darth Vader that everybody wants to be—it’s his supernatural mentor, Obi-Wan Kenobi. The character has always been a fan favorite, but this year he’s especially hot thanks to his very own spin-off series streaming on Disney Plus.

Obi-Wan has truly stolen the show in nearly every iteration of the franchise. The legendary Jedi Master is known for his knoble power skills, training Anakin Skywalker (before he became known as Darth Vader)as his padawan in the prequel trilogy, where he also rodeo-ed giant lizards and saved the galaxy from killer robots, and later mentoring Luke Skywalker, teaching him how to use the Force in the very first Star Wars movie. 

If you want to dress up as this fan-favorite character, we found everything you need to be the best Jedi this Halloween. Luckily, Obi-Wan’s look is a cross between cozy and magical, so you can pull off an intricate costume while still remaining comfy all night. Pretty much all you need is an old-school wizard robe, some baggy clothing that’s good for both meditating and fighting bad guys, and a couple of accessories to make this look complete.
