Jennifer Jones, also known as Jennifer Jones Simon, is an American actress and mental health activist. In a career spanning more than 5 decades, she has been nominated for an Oscar 5 times, including once winning the Best Actress award and once winning a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress. Jones is one of the youngest actresses to receive an Oscar, which she won on her 25th birthday. Born in Tulsa, Oklahoma, Jones was a model at a young age before moving on to actress, appearing in two successive films in 1939. Her third role was the lead role of Bernadette Soubirous in The Song of Bernadette, earning her a Oscar and a Golden Globe for Best Actress of the Year. She went on to star in a number of critically acclaimed films and added three more Oscar nominations in the mid-1940s, including Since He Left, Love Letter and Duel in the Sun ..
