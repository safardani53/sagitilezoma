Kelly Brook stuns with tousled hair in a shot for her official 2021 calendar

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, February 7, 2024

KELLY Brook goes for a bit of root and branch reform — by posing with tousled hair in a wooden shack.

But Kelly, 41, will have twigged that change is probably the last thing she needs — so the model sticks to what she does best in this stunning shot for her official 2021 calendar.

It comes 20 years after her first — and they get better every year.

The calendar is available from danilo.com.

The model, 41, also poses in a blue bikini in the calendar which is sure to get fans hoping the months go by as fast as possible.

Opening up about finally feeling comfortable in her own skin, she told The Sun: “I feel in my twenties I was super ambitious and living my life having an amazing time.

“In my thirties I was stressing about having children, getting married, where I am in my career, where I’m going to live.

“In your forties it’s fine, you’re alive, you got a job, and you’ve got a boyfriend.”
