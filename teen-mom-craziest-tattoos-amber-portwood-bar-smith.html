See Teen Mom stars' craziest tattoos from Amber Portwood's massive portrait of daughter Leah to Bar

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

JADE Cline's new face tattoo is just the latest ink in a long line of Teen Mom tatts.

Jade revealed a small lightning bolt placed next to her ear in a video on her Instagram Stories on Sunday.

Jade made the revelation when asked by a fan during an Instagram Q&A session: "Any tattoos? If so, how many and what's your favorite?"

The MTV star replied: "Lost count. But my face tat is one of my fav [sic]."

The 24-year-old Teen Mom 2 star has dozens of tattoos, including a "Bite Me" inking on her arm, which she had done in November last year.

She also has her four-year-old daughter Kloie's name inked on her, as well as multiple skulls and roses.

Jade is just one of the many Teem Mom stars to ink up - some for better and some for worse.

Take a look through some of the most memorable Teen Mom tattoos.

MACI BOOKOUT'S BACK TATTOO

Maci Bookout has the honor of having what fans call the worst tattoos of any of the Teen Moms.

The 30-year-old scored especial scorn for her “shoulder to cry on tissue” and her back “piece of paper”  tattoos.

The ripped up sheet of paper looks like something a student in high school would cram into their backpack.

It takes up her entire back, and comes with the caption/mantra: "Learn to Fee."

Many Reddit users mistook the tissue on her shoulder for a “clam”, a “weird bat wing”, a “ghost” and a “superhero cape," instead of its true design as a hankie for a shoulder to cry on.

One critic summarized the tattoos as: “Maci's back tattoo is one hundred percent the worst tattoo I have ever seen, and I am a connoisseur of bad tattoos.

It looks like something I would have drawn in middle school when I was feeling angsty."

JENELLE EVANS "YOLO"

Jenelle Evans may only live once, but her self-confessed "dumbest tattoo" will live on.

In February 2021, the 30-year-old took to TikTok to share a clip that kicks off with a woman asking viewers what is the "dumbest tattoo" they've gotten.

The short video then shows the ex MTV star showing off a black and gray cursive writing tattoo of the once famous motto started by rapper Drake: "YOLO".

Jenelle captioned it: "LMFAO, had to share this tattoo ASAP."

AMBER PORTWOOD'S DAUGHTER

In 2010, Amber Portwood revealed what has been described as a "creepy" tribute to daughter Leah - a lifesize, full color face wrapped around her stomach.

The tattoo, which seems to feature a young Leah looking worried, was ridiculed online.

On Reddit, one user shared: “Sorry, sorry… You're telling me, that tattoo of Leahs face… Is on Ambers body? Take it away, I can't stand to look,”

BAR SMITH'S FACE

Regrets? Bar Smith has had a few.

Especially his two facial tattoos, right above each eyebrow.

In an attempt to "clean up his image" following a series of arrests and an addiction to alcohol, Bar, 24, has undergone at least four laser removal sessions.

Bar reportedly wants to be a vet tech one day and recently learned that facial tattoos specifically are frowned upon at school.

In late November, he updated the results, telling fans, "It's a long process but they are fading."

TYLER BALTIERRA'S PHALLIC BATMAN

Tyler Baltierra was trying to express his love for two special people in his life in 2019 - His new daughter, Vaeda, and Batman.

So the 29-year-old Teen Mom OG star, and husband to Catelynn Lowell, got an everlasting tribute to both.

Tyler's special tattoo features the dark knight cradling the tiny newborn in a loving embrace.

But many Reddit users though the extended appearance of Batman's cowl looked more like something that would make boy wonder sidekick Dick Grayson blush.

"This is a dick," one user explained. "This picture is of a dick. There's even an opening for the urethra, and the line for the start of the head. Everything."

Tyler has since scrubbed the photo from his Instagram, but fans continue to give him a hard time about it.

KAILYN LOWERY'S NIGHTMARE

Kailyn Lowry got a huge dreamcatcher tattoo on her thigh in 2020, to reflect her Native American roots - only to find out she's not.

The Teen Mom 2 star was left infuriated when a DNA ancestry test revealed she has no Native American heritage from her dad's side as she'd previously thought.

During a visit to her aunt and father in Texas, the aunt told he a convincing story about how she is Native American. .

"So I get this huge dreamcatcher tattoo on my thigh because I'm like, I'm f**king Native American, this is amazing! I know where I came from," she told PEOPLE at the time.

Years later, two different DNA tests proved the reality star's aunt wrong.

"I have no Native American in me whatsoever. None. Not even a one per cent," Kailyn said, laughing."

JAVI'S TATTOO UNDO

Javi Marroquin's romantic gesture to show his undying love for his Teen Mom 2 girlfriend Briana DeJesus was to get matching tattoos.  

The couple went with a chess theme, with Javi getting a king chess piece inked on his ribcage, while Briana opted for a queen.

But three months later, it was game over for the pair, as they broke up.

Javi moved on to a relationship with Lauren Comeau, and in March 2019, on their first anniversary, covered up the tattoo, and shared a special message on Instagram:
