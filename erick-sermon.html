Erick Sermon Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, October 1, 2024

Erick is also one of the celebrities who love to keep his personal profile low. He is about to touch his 50s, but till date there is no any information about his love affairs and married life. It seems like he is still living a single life.

Brick has been active in music since 1987. Initially he also recorded several solo albums and singles. In 1993, he signed a recording deal with Def Jam and began recording his debut solo album. His solo debut album, No Pressure was finally released on 19th of October 1993. His second album, Double or Nothing was released at the end of 1995. He began his music career as one of the member of hip hop group EPMD. He rejoined them in 1997. His third album, Erick Onasis was released in 2000. His net worth is said to be around $7 million dollars.
