Kim Kardashian caught out in 'lie' as she shares Idaho lake getaway pics with North, Saint, Chicago,

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, May 4, 2024

FANS have called Kim Kardashian out on a lie after the reality star shared a throwback post with her four kids.

The Kardashians star took to Instagram on Monday with a photo carousel of a family getaway to the lake.

Kim, 43, uploaded a few photos of herself enjoying various activities in an undisclosed setting with her kids: North, 10, Chicago, 5, Saint, 7, and Psalm, 4.

Kim shares her four children with her ex-husband Kanye West, 46.

The family was seen spending time on a boat and walking through scenic settings, seemingly during a summer month.

Kim sported her long, dark brown locks and curvy figure, while her kids all looked much younger than they currently do.

Simply writing "Lake Life" in her caption, followers took to her comments section to call out the Hulu star for sharing photos that appeared to be roughly five years old.

One person wrote: "This looks like a couple years ago based on how the kids look."

"i thought the same," a second agreed.

A third chimed in: "This is like 5 years ago."

While some thought Kim should've specified the timeline of the post, others stepped in to defend the reality star.

"Love this!!! And who cares when this was for all the people worried about how long ago this is," one wrote.

FAMILY LIES

Most recently, Kim's sister Khloe Kardashian, 39, was similarly called out by fans in an Instagram post.

The reality star took to the platform on Sunday to commemorate her time at a recent Mariah Carey concert with family.

Khloe shared a photo alongside the All I Want For Christmas Is You singer, along with Kim and their mom Kris Jenner, 67.

Also present at the concert were Khloe's daughter True, 5, and nieces Dream, 7, North, and Chicago.

In the caption, the Good American co-founder declared it was the girls' first-ever concert.

"The Queen of Christmas!!!! For the little girls very FIRST concert ever, we went to see the Queen herself, @mariahcarey !!" she wrote.

"We all had the best time, creating the most magical memories!! Thank you mommy for taking all of us!"

Some fans called her out in the comments section, questioning if it really was their first show.

"How is it their first concert when their uncles are Kanye West, Travis Barker and Travis Scott lol," one person wrote.

Blink-182 drummer Travis Barker, 47, is the husband of Kourtney Kardashian 44, while rapper Travis Scott, 32, is the ex of Kylie Jenner, 26.

With all three being in the music industry, other followers chimed in to agree.
