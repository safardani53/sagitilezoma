See Her Best Red Carpet Looks Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, September 9, 2024

Miranda Lambert turns 37 on November 10, and it’s no secret that it’s been quite a year for the country superstar! Since her last birthday, Miranda and her hubby, Brendan McLoughlin, have lit up social media with their love-fest after their secret wedding ceremony in January 2019. Miranda has been gushing about her love non-stop, as she’s been promoting her 2019 album, Wildcard. What better way to top off her Wildcard era than with a birthday celebration? Of course, we want to celebrate Miranda’s birthday, too, so we’ve rounded up some of her best red carpet looks of all time.

After so many years in the country music industry, Miranda has become a pro on red carpets. She attends the CMA Awards and ACM Awards almost every year, and has made a number of appearances at the Grammy Awards throughout her career, as well. Mixed in with those are other country music events, and Miranda looks amazing at every single one she attends! During the 2019 summer, she was honored at the ACM Honors event in Nashville, and she looked incredible while dressed for the occasion in a black blazer dress, which showed off her toned and tanned legs.

Before that, Miranda attended the ACM Awards in April 2019, and she was a glowing goddess in her bright green dress. The ensemble hugged her in all the right places and showed off her stunning figure, and Miranda looked fierce as she posed for photos — both by herself AND with her man! Her gown, designed by Alex Perry, totally stood out on the arrival carpet. Miranda accessorized her look with Emily P. Wheeler and Brent Neale jewelry along with a Benedetta Bruzziches clutch and Casadei shoes! Just months after dazzling in her gorgeous green gown, Miranda hit the red carpet of the 53rd Annual CMA Awards on November 13, 2019 — just a few days after her birthday! She wore this stunning fuchsia gown with intricate detailing all along the fabric. Miranda’s dress almost looked as if it had fireworks designed all over it! The dress beautifully accentuated Miranda’s figure, and she totally rocked the red carpet with all of the confidence in the world.

There are plenty of more amazing red carpet looks where these came from, though. Scroll through the gallery above to check out Miranda’s hottest red carpet looks of all time!
