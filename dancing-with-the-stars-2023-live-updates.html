Who was eliminated last week?

So far, the eliminated contestants for Dancing with the Stars season 32 include:

During the premiere episode's conclusion, Matt Walsh and Koko Iwasaki were sent home after performing the cha-cha-cha.

"I am SO proud of Matt and so proud of how he danced tonight with some real chacha steps," Koko said of her and Matt's elimination.

She told her Instagram followers: "He’s a joy of a human, a true student, and I would have him as a partner every season if I could."

Fans wish Artem well

Multiple DWTS fans have taken to the show’s X post announcing that Artem is out tonight due to Covid-19.

One fan commented: “Get well Artem!!! Praying for quick and healthy recovery! [heart emoji]”

Another wrote: “Noooo Artem is my fav pro! Get well soon!”

More on Artem not competing

Artem wished the two good luck, while Ezra wrote: "Gonna make you proud Artem! We got this @charitylawson."

Charity chimed in: "A duo if you ever seen what! Grateful for @ezra.sosa stepping in and getting the job done!!! Don’t miss tonight and make sure to VOTE for me!"

Taking to her Instagram Story, the former Bachelorette also noted that Artem has "been the best through it all."

Artem is not competing tonight

Due to testing positive for Covid-19, professional dancer Artem Chigvintsev, 41, will no longer be performing with his partner, Charity Lawson, 27.

Artem took to social media to inform his followers of the news, telling them that Charity and Ezra have done an "amazing job" and have been working "really hard."

The official Instagram account for DWTS also shared the announcement, which garnered comments from the stars involved.
