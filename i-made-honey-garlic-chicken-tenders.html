I made honey garlic chicken tenders in the Air Fryer and the results were amazing

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 9, 2024

MANY people have tried and failed to make the perfect crispy friend chicken in an Air Fryer.

For some the chicken loses it's crispiness when it is not fried in the conventional way.

Others are simply fried chicken purists how refuse to believe that the the popular takeaway dish could possibly be cooked in such an unconventional way.

A self confessed foodie has run the risk of the wrath of fried chicken lovers every where by claiming she can make the most crispiest flavoursome chicken tenders that could potentially rival any fast food outlet.

Using the handle Veryhungrygreek, the chicken lover shared her recipe with her 124.4k followers.

Christina started the video by teasing us with some that she had mad earlier.

More on food shopping

Smiling she showed her fans a plate of crispy honey drenched chicken.

She says to camera: "Honey, garlic chicken tenders for only 361 calories yes that's the whole plate and also 43 grams of protein."

To make these bad boys she started by slicing the chicken breast fillets and seasoning them with rock salt and cracked black pepper.

She then added one teaspoon of paprika and then dipped the seasoned breasts into an egg wash.

Most read in Fabulous

Then it was time to add the secret ingredient that she was swears would give the chicken it's crispiness.

And the secret ingredient was... coating the tenders with 30 grams of crushed cornflakes.

Christina then added the cornflake coated goujons to the Air fryer and 15 minutes later, she showcased a tray of mouth wateringly good looking plate of chicken pieces.

Which she then drenched in honey and spring onion to garnish.

Fans flocked to her page to comment on the delicious pieces of chicken.

Victoria Jayne Tucke posted: “Just made this!!We made it in smaller chunks and my daughter loved it.”
