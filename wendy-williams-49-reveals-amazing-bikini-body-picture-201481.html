Wendy Williams Bikini Body Picture: Star, 49, Shares Sexy Vacation Pic

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, March 17, 2024

Whoa, Wendy Williams! The talk show host wore nothing but a revealing bikini in an Instagram snapshot taken during a vacation in a gorgeous, tropical destination with husband Kevin Hunter. "I know it's FREEZING, but I'll be BACK TOMORROW with all NEW SHOWS and JUICY, JUICY #HOTTOPICS…get ready…I AM!!! Happy New Year!" she wrote in the caption.

Wearing geometric-printed bikini bottoms and a busty, black top, 49-year-old Williams gave her best swimsuit model pose, bending to her right in the crystal clear waters of the locale. The talk show host revealed her flat tummy, muscular legs and large tattoo scrawled across her waist in the pretty shot.

At nearly 50 years old, the TV personality later updated her Facebook page to address critics who questioned whether or not it was truly Williams in the bikini shot. "Thanks for the love & hate, but people that's ME!" she revealed. "No filter, photoshop or nothing…taken from my husband's cell phone an hr ago…sorry haters."
