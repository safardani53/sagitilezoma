What is David Tennants net worth? The US Sun

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, October 9, 2024

OVER the years David Tennant has become a much-loved actor, with millions of fans around the world.

The talented star cemented himself is a household name after he took on the iconic role of Doctor Who.

Who is David Tennant?

David Tennant was born David John McDonald in Bathgate, West Lothian, Scotland, on April 18, 1971.

As well as being a movie and TV star, he is also known for his work with the Royal Shakespeare Company.

However, he best known for being the tenth Time Lord on Doctor Who, replacing Christopher Eccleston.

His first full episode as Doctor Who was the 2005 festive special, The Christmas Invasion.

He played the role until January 1, 2010, when he bowed out to make way for Matt Smith.

But, much to the delight of fans, David returned to Doctor Who as the fourteenth Time Lord in November 2023.

Who is David Tennant's wife Georgia?

David is married to actress Georgia Moffett.

The pair met on the set of Doctor Who episode The Doctor’s Daughter.

What is David Tennant's net worth?

David is estimated to have a net worth of around £7M.

What TV shows has David Tennant starred in?

Away from Doctor Who, David is best known for starring in ITV drama Broadchurch, alongside Olivia Colman.

He also starred in the BBC show There She Goes.

Elsewhere and David played villain Kilgrave in the Marvel and Netflix series Jessica Jones.

The star took on the tough role of playing serial killer Dennis Nilsen in the drama Des.

In December 2022, Tennant starred as Alexander Litvinenko in the ITV's Litvinenko.

What films has David Tennant appeared in?

Just before landing the role of Doctor Who, David became well known to Harry Potter fans when he was cast as Barty Crouch Jnr in The Goblet of Fire.

In 2009, he starred as Sir Piers Pomfrey in St Trinian’s 2: The Legend of Fritton’s Gold and the following year voiced the character of Spitelout in How to Train Your Dragon.

David starred as vampire expert Peter Vincent in the Fright Night remake and in 2014, he starred alongside Rosamund Pike and Billy Connolly in What We Did on Our Holiday.

The actor also starred in You, Me and Him, which was co-produced by his wife Georgia Moffett.
