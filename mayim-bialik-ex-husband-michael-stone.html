Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, March 31, 2024

MAYIM BIALIK has had an impressive career as an actress, a neuroscientist, and a quiz show host.

Though her professional achievements are well documented, fans may have questions over Mayim Bialik's personal life - including her marriage to ex-husband Michael Stone.

Who is Mayim Bialik's ex-husband Michael Stone?

Mayim Bialik was married to Michael Stone from 2003 to 2013.

The businessman met his future wife in a calculus class while they were both at grad school at UCLA.

Stone was raised as a Mormon but converted to Judaism for Mayim.

The actress previously revealed that on her very first date with Michael she told him she could only marry him if he was Jewish.
