Primark launches a gorgeous 8 heart-shaped bag almost exactly like Radley's 99 sell-out

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

HEART-SHAPED bags are just about the cutest way to incorporate a pop of colour into your summer wardrobe.

But if you weren’t one of the lucky shoppers who snapped up this sell-out heart clutch bag from Radley then you can now get your cuteness fix with this amazing dupe from Primark.

In a gorgeous shade of cherry red, this clutch bag has really set our hearts racing - better still, it only costs £8

With a long gold chain making it perfect for cross-body wear, this cutesy little bag is the easiest way to liven up a simple summer outfit.

Pair with white summer dresses for feminine overload or combine with a black leather jacket to add a girly touch.

First to kick off the heart-shaped trend was this irresistible heart-shaped bag from Radley, which is currently sold out online.

The luxury leather brand is known for sweet designs and signature logo of a miniature Scottish terrier.

Yet at a bank-breaking £99 for a simple (yet adorable) red clutch, we prefer to get the look for less with this total steal of a Primark bag.

Let's just consider our staple summer accessory sorted.
