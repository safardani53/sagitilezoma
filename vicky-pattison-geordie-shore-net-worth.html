Who is Vicky Pattison and whats her net worth? The Sun

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, March 6, 2024

VICKY Pattison continues to see her star

The TV personality has got her love life back on track too after moving in with boyfriend Ercan Ramadan. Here is all you need to know about her...

Who is Vicky Pattison?

Vicky Pattison was born on November 16, 1987, in Newcastle, England, making her 34-years-old.

Before finding fame she worked on the door of some of Newcastle's nightclubs.

She rose to fame on MTV's Geordie Shore.

Since then she's appeared on multiple reality shows and has her own podcast Vicky Pattison: The Secret To.

What is Vicky Pattison's net worth?

The Sun exclusively revealed by the end of 2021 Vicky had raked in her first million pound pay check.

She has raked in around half the cash from lucrative Instagram posts.

The TV star paid close to £200K to the taxman meaning with costs and expenses taken off she’s turned over more than £1m.

Who is Vicky Pattison dating now?

In November, 2018, Vicky called it quits with her fiancé John Noble after he was caught fooling around with another woman at a Dubai nightclub.

The Sun exclusively revealed how the reality star immediately shelved her wedding plans and wasted no time unfollowing her former beau from her social media platforms.

Vicky is now happily engaged to Ercan Ramadan - who she started dating in early 2019.

Ercan, who found fame in Towie in 2016, lives with Vicky in Essex.

As well as Towie The Only Way Is Essex, Ercan has also starred in The Only Way Is Marbs.

When was Vicky Pattison on Geordie Shore?

Before the Geordie Shore house opened its doors to eight unsuspecting party animals in 2011, no one knew who Vicky was.

She rocketed to fame with her signature straight-talking on the show which also starred Gary Beadle, Charlotte Crosby and Holly Hagan.

Vicky left the show in 2014 and took part in two seasons of explicit dating show Ex On The Beach which constantly hit the headlines for its contestants' outrageous behaviour..

What else has Vicky Pattison done?

Vicky is a regular panellist on Loose Women, and has made guest appearances on This Morning, Celebrity Dinner Date, Celebrity Juice and Virtually Famous.

In 2015 Vicky won I'm A Celebrity, beating runner-up George Shelley.

She also appeared in Channel 4's relaunch of The Crystal Maze in 2017.

In the same year, she pulled out of All Star Driving School without taking her test and was quickly caught in Channel 4's Celebrity Hunted.

In 2019, she joined the all-star line-up of Celebrity MasterChef, where she made it to the final but lost out to Greg Rutherford.

Following on from her MasterChef success Vicky joined E4's Celebrity Coach Trip 2019 with former Love Island star Scott Thomas.

In December 2020 she took part in Celebrity MasterChef: Christmas Cook-Off, but was pipped to the post again by Janet Street-Porter.

In August 2021, she competed in Celebrity SAS: Who Dares Wins.

Since 2020 Vicky has been hosting a very successful podcast called Vicky Pattison: The Secret To.

In August 2022 she fronted the Channel 4 documentary, Vicky Pattison: My Dad, Alcohol and Me.

Who is Vicky's father John?

Vicky us the daughter of 62-year-olf John Pattison.

She has revealed that as a child, her dad battled with alcoholism.

Speaking on Instagram about her father's battle with alcohol abuse she said: "My dad's illness & my own complicated relationship with alcohol is something that has had a profound affect on who I am as a person.

"At times, when I wasn't in the best place- when I was abusing alcohol & depending on it I would lie awake at night wondering if this was just it- the start of that slippery slope.

"And in my darkest moments, I truly believed that it was just a forgone conclusion- that no matter how hard I tried to be something different, something more or something better... "
