BBC Breakfast viewers left stunned as Naga Munchetty and Charlie Stayt are joined by an unexpected g

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

NAGA Munchetty and Charlie Stayt had a third member on the sofa this morning.

BBC viewers were left open-mouthed as a brand new guest stole the limelight from Naga and Charlie.

The hosts could be seen grinning as they were greeted by an unlikely third presenter - fluffy Siberian Husky 'Thunder'.

Thunder appeared on the sofa alongside his owner Adrien, to share his unbelievable gift.

The special pooch, nicknamed 'Thunder the wonder dog' has his very own job - which is to comfort those patients with complex needs in care homes.

He also works as a therapy dog to support those in palliative care.

More on BBC Breakfast

Featured on the show, Thunder could be seen comforting his friend and patient Ben, who has Epilepsy and Cerebral Palsy.

Thunder accompanied Ben in bed where he appeared to comfort and assist him with physiotherapy.

The amazing husky was later seen cuddling up to Charlie and Naga as Adrien explained the importance of his work and also how Thunder is sensitive to people he meets and is even left upset after the death of a patient.

Adrien described that when previously visiting a care home, Thunder laid on a gentleman's bed, who unfortunately later that day passed away, and appeared to comfort him.
