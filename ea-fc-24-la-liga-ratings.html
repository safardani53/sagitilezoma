EA Sports FC 24 ranks the best players in Spains La Liga and Liga F

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

EA SPORTS FC 24 has unveiled the best-rated players in Spanish football’s top leagues, La Liga and women’s Liga F.

The ranking comes after EA Sports FC 24 ranked the best Premier League players yesterday, and the global top 24 earlier this week.

EA Sports FC is the new name of long-running football sim video game series FIFA, which has rebranded after publisher EA Sports and football’s top governing body ended their partnership.

The next game in the series, EA Sports FC 24, will be released on September 29, 2023, for PlayStation and Xbox consoles, PC, and the Nintendo Switch.

La Liga’s fresh ranking of best players in the inaugural EA Sports FC 24 game puts FC Barcelona’s Lewandowski and Real Madrid’s Courtois at the top, with a shared 90 rating.

The now-legendary stars are followed by Barcelona goalie Marc-André Ter Stegen and Real Madrid winger Vinícius, who are both rated 89.

The Real Madrid-Barcelona rating domination is first broken by Atlético de Madrid’s Griezmann and Jan Oblak, who together with Fede Valverde from Real Madrid receive a rating of 88.

At 88, Fede Valverde is also getting one of the biggest upgrades compared to his FIFA 23 rating. 

Luka Modrić and Frenkie de Jong come next, rated 87, and English star Jude Bellingham makes his debut in La Liga with an impressive 86 rating.

Bellingham shares the 86 rating with FC Barcelona’s Araujo, João Cancelo, Pedri, and Gündoğan, Real Madrid’s Toni Kroos and Éder Militão, and Villarreal’s Dani Parejo.

The player ratings for Liga F, Spain’s top-level women’s football competition, are dominated by FC Barcelona players.

Barça Femení players occupy half of the top 24, including the first 11 spots.

Liga F’s ranking is topped by Barça midfielder and current FIFA Women’s World Cup champion Alexia Putellas, rated an impressive 91.

Putellas's overall rating is one point higher than that of La Liga's top stars, Lewandowski and Courtiois.

She is followed by fellow teammates Aitana Bonmatí and Hansen, sharing a 90 rating.
