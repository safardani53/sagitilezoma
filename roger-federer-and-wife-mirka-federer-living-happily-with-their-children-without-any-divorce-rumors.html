Roger Federer and Wife Mirka Federer Living Happily with their Children without any divorce rumors

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, March 15, 2024

We have seen and read many controversies in a relationship. There are few relationships that last for a long and today we are talking about Roger Federer and Mirka Federer who formally are husband and wife and are living a happy married life.

The fans of pro tennis might be thinking about Roger Federer's twins and about Roger's married life. So, let's know more about his twin's daughter and son and also about his blissful married life, which is without any divorce rumors.

Mirka and Roger Federer relationship

We all know that Roger is a professional tennis player, but did you know Mirka is also a professional tennis player? Their same profession made them closer and helped them know each other better.

Mirka first met Roger at the 2000 Sydney Olympics, where both of them were competing.

Mirka and Roger shared their first kiss on the last day of the 2000 Summer Olympics in Sydney.

Federer never shies off to praise his wife. He says,

"Mirka has been an amazing support for me. She's the best."

How long has been Mirka married to Roger Federer?

Mirka first met Roger at the 2000 Sydney Olympics, where both of them were competing. After nine years of their relation, they tied the knot at Wenkenhof Villa in Basel in April 2009. 

The couple's celebrated their wedding ceremony in a simple manner. They only invited close friends and family to their wedding.

Federer is a family guy and wants his family to be around him whenever possible. He rented a home for his family to stay in when he plays at Wimbledon. He bought two homes: one for his family and one for him and his team. Each home was worth $6.2 million and he paid a total of $46,000 to rent for two weeks.

Mirka and Roger Federer's Children

Twin Daughter

The couple became a parent for the first time when Mirka gave birth to twin daughters in July 2009, Myla Rose and Charlene Riva.

An amazing and interesting fact!! Giving birth to the twins in Federer's family seems to be hereditary. Even Roger's sister, Diana has two sets of her own, a boy and a girl. And not only this, for the second time Roger and Mirka Federer became a parent of a twin.

Twin Son

After five years of the birth of their twin daughter, Mirka hit the jackpot again, and this time she gave birth to twin sons, Leo, and Lenny.

When you give birth to twins once, you become happy and surprised, but when you give birth to twin again, how do you feel? Shocked?? We don't know about you, but we surely were startled when we found out that Federer again became a father of a twin for the second time.

Federer expressed his happiness in an interview, He said,

"“This is the best time of my life, and when we found out we were having twins, it was like one of those moments where you’re like: ‘Wow, I can’t believe it, it’s really happening again.’”

Roger got huge support from her wife during his career

Roger Federer is a legend. His hard work and support from his wife made him reach that level. As there is a saying "Behind every success of a man there is a great role of a woman."
