Nicki Minaj Performs at Bar Mitzvah, Fulfills Fantasties of Multiple 13-Year Old Boys

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, June 6, 2024

Justin Bieber and Nicki Minaj were all about unexpected wish fulfillment this past weekend.

First, as previously detailed, Bieber sent students into hysterics on Saturday night when he crashed a prom in Los Angeles.

Then – not to be outdone in the surprising attendance department – Minaj was the guest of honor at Matt Murstein’s bar mitzvah, posing with various young boys in one of our favorite photos in the history of the Internet:

Matt’s father is Andrew Murstein.

The founder, president, and largest shareholder of Medallion Financial Corp. (a publicly traded company with a about $259 million market cap), Andrew likely shelled out a pretty penny for Minaj’s appearance.

But it was totally worth it! At least for us.

Minaj performed a set of seven songs at the religious event, captioning the amazing picture above with the following message:

"Get a load of these little hunks I met last night @ the Bar Mitzvah. They were very ummm turnT."

According to various videos shared on Twitter soon after Nicki’s performance, the artist told the teens at the reception to “get an education, stay in school and don’t be a bum.”

She also addressed the females in the room only and said:

"Never let a man have to take care of you. Do you understand me? Be your own woman. Be your own person."

We love it. Up next, we expect to see Kim Kardashian paid to attend some baby’s bris.
