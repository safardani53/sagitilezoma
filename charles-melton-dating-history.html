Look At Charles Meltons High Profile Dating History

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

Charles Melton, renowned for his role as Reggie Mantle on the hit TV series Riverdale, hasn’t only left an indelible mark on the screen but also enchanted fans with his impressive dating history.

The 32-year-old star has not only garnered attention for his on-screen charisma but has also been a subject of curiosity due to his relationships with some high-profile leading ladies. 

From his on-again, off-again relationship with fellow Riverdale cast Camila Mendes to his relationships with Chase Sui Wonders and Chloe Bennet, Melton has a marvelous dating history.

While the actor generally keeps a low profile about his personal life, he has expressed a profound belief in the transformative power of love. 

As Melton once shared with Hunger Magazine, “Life’s too short to not be a hopeless romantic. If you can believe in anything right now, believe in love.”

A rewind at Melton’s dating history!

Charles Melton dated fellow ‘Riverdale’ co-star Camila Mendes

Melton and Mendes first crossed paths on the set of the popular television series Riverdale season 2. Shortly, the pair translated their on-camera chemistry into a real-life romance. 

The confirmation of their relationship came in October 2018, when Mendes took to Instagram to share a cute photo of Melton giving a gentle kiss on her forehead and captioned, “Mine.” 

Further, the couple debuted the red carpet at the 2018 GQ Men of the Year Party and graced the 2019 Met Gala as each other’s dates.

Charles Melton and his former girlfriend Camila Mendes. (Source: Twitter)

Moreover, Mendes attended the premiere of Melton’s romantic film, The Sun Is Also A Star, and couldn’t help but gush about Melton’s romantic gestures, admitting that he was a “hopeless romantic,” in an interview with Entertainment Tonight. 

The actress also shared that Melton often expressed his love through handwritten letters, adding a touch of old-school charm to their modern-day romance.

The couple marked their first anniversary in August 2019 as Mendes took to Instagram once again to share a snapshot of them kissing on a boat. The caption read, “365 days. I love you.” 

However, in December 2019, Melton and Mendes decided to take a break. Despite a brief period of reconciliation rumors in June 2021, the rekindling of their romance proved short-lived. 

The couple eventually went their separate ways, with Mendes gracefully moving on and finding companionship with her Música co-star Rudy Mancuso. 

Charles Melton moved on with Chase Sui Wonders

After his high-profile relationship with actress Mendes, Melton moved on with rising star Wonders. The romance rumors between Melton and Wonders began in March 2022, as the duo was frequently spotted together. 

Choosing a more discreet approach, the couple was able to maintain a low profile in the following months, which fueled rumors about the nature of their relationship. 

Charles Melton and his former girlfriend Chase Sui Wonders. (Source: Twitter)

However, in July 2022, Melton took to Instagram to share a snapshot that confirmed their relationship. The picture featured the couple cozily leaning into each other, both wearing matching Brooklyn Cyclones hats, while enjoying a baseball game. 

Similarly, Melton showed his support for Wonders at a screening of her movie Bodies Bodies Bodies later in the same month. However, the couple eventually broke up. 

Charles Melton was recently in a relationship with Chloe Bennet

In May 2023, the tabloid headlines buzzed with speculation as Melton, the actor known for his role in Riverdale, found himself at the center of a rumored romance with Bennet.

The dating rumors sparked when eagle-eyed fans spotted the duo strolling through the streets of Beverly Hills, with Melton wrapping his arm around the Agents of S.H.I.E.L.D. actress. 

An insider reported that the couple had been “quietly dating for a few months,” according to People Magazine.

Charles Melton and his former girlfriend Chloe Bennet. (Source: Twitter)

Similarly, after a few months in August 2023, the pair was captured at Taylor Swift’s highly Eras Tour in Inglewood, California.

However, the couple seemed to have quietly parted ways, leaving fans speculating about the reasons behind the apparent split. 

Recently, Bennet was spotted engaging in public displays of affection with The 1975 bassist Ross MacDonald in November 2023.

Is Charles Melton dating anyone now?

As of December 2023, it appears that Melton is possibly single. A look at his Instagram profile doesn’t provide any hints or clues that would suggest his involvement in a romantic relationship at the moment.

Reports emerged in May 2023 linking him romantically to actress Bennet. However, despite initial speculations, the couple managed to keep their relationship relatively low-key. However, People Magazine suggested that Bennet has since moved on.

Charles Melton’s brief bio

Melton was born on January 4, 1991, to his parents, Sukyong and Phil Melton. His father’s affiliation with the military-led the family to move frequently. 

They spent a significant period in Pyeongtaek, Gyeonggi, South Korea, due to his father’s military assignments. Eventually settling down in Manhattan, Kansas, Melton graduated from Manhattan High School in 2009. 

He later attended Kansas State University, where he played as a defensive back on the football team under the guidance of Coach Bill Snyder. 

However, at the age of 20, driven by a passion for acting, Melton decided to move to Los Angeles in 2012 to pursue acting.

Before landing acting jobs, Melton worked as a dog walker through the app Wag. His initial success came as a model, securing coveted gigs with renowned brands such as Dolce & Gabbana, Kenneth Cole, and MAC. 

His acting career took off with notable guest appearances on popular shows like Glee and American Horror Story. In 2017, he garnered widespread recognition when he assumed the role of Reggie Mantle in the second season of the CW series Riverdale, replacing Ross Butler. 

Similarly, he made his feature film debut in 2019, portraying Daniel Bae in The Sun Is Also a Star, the film adaptation of Nicola Yoon’s novel. 

Recently, Melton co-starred in May December, a film alongside Natalie Portman and Julianne Moore, which was released on November 17, 2023.
