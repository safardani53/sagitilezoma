Pics Of The French Singer Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

Jeanne Louise Galice, better known by her stage name Jain, is known across the world. The French singer, who grew up in Abu Dhabi, the Congo-Brazzabille, and Dubai, broke out in 2015 with her ‘Hope’ EP. The single, ‘Come,’ blew up and a year after release, it was certified Diamond. She released her debut album, ‘Zanaka,’ in November 2015. It went Platinum three-times in France by January 2017. The success of this release saw her perform all over the globe, including a 2017 appearance on ‘The Late Show with Stephen Colbert.’ She rleased her second studio album, ‘Souldier,’ in August 2018. It went to No. 1 in France, and was certified platinum afterward. The singer, who combines the music influences of all the places where she grew up into a distinctive sound, was given a high honor in 2019: she was picked to perform at the Opening Ceremony of the FIFA Women’s World Cup.
