New iPhone price UK here's how much the iPhone XS, XS Max and XR cost

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, February 23, 2024

APPLE unveiled its latest range of iPhones at a special event at its California HQ on September 12.

The iPhone XS is the flagship model for 2018, replacing (and borrowing its design from) last year's popular iPhone X.

What are the UK prices for the new iPhones?

The iPhone XS with a 5.8-inch screen is available with 64GB, 256GB and 512GB.

The price ranges are £999, £1,149 and £1,349.

Prices of the bigger iPhone XS Max are £1,099 for 64GB, £1,249 for 256GB and £1,449 for 512GB.

The tech giant has also launched the iPhone XR.

It’s separate from the core iPhone XS series and is designed as a cheaper option, with a less premium LCD screen.

For 64GB you’ll pay £749, for 128GB £799 and for 256GB £899.

What is the biggest and most expensive iPhone?

The 512GB iPhone XS Max now becomes the biggest and most expensive iPhone ever.

It has a 6.5-inch screen - the biggest ever featured on an iPhone.

The iPhone XS has a 5.8inch screen.

Both feature Face ID facial recognition technology.

The phones come in three different colours — silver, gold and space gray.

Apple is also kitting its iPhone XS out with the latest iOS 12 software update.

This includes a new feature called Siri Shortcuts, that lets you carry out multiple tasks in a single command.

Apple has also significantly upgraded the camera system for the new iPhone XS.

On the back of the iPhone XS, there's a 12-megapixel wide-angle camera paired up with a 12-megapixel telephoto lens, working in a dual-snapper system.

What features does the XR have?

This new iPhone is the direct successor of 2017's iPhone 8 and is designed as a cheaper option, rocking a less premium LCD screen.

But it does boast Face ID facial recognition, just like the pricier iPhone XS and iPhone XS Max.

Despite being a more budget model, it's got many high-end features – including the swanky all-screen design introduced with last year's iPhone X.

It has a large, notched 6.1-inch screen, with a 1792 x 828-pixel resolution.

The phone is available in the following colours: White, Black, Blue, Yellow, Coral, and (Product)Red.

This model will come later than the iPhone XS – pre-orders open on October 19, before shipping on October 26.
