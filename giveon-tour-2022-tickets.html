Givon tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 29, 2024

GIVĒON is known as a popular R&B singer and songwriter who first rose to fame after collaborating with Drake on the 2020 single, Chicago Freestyle.

Now, the rising star is hitting the road for a new North American tour.

If you click on a link in this story we may earn affiliate revenue.

Givēon tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Tickets for Givēon's tour are scheduled to go on sale on June 17, 2022, starting at 10am local time.

Pre-sale tickets will also be available on June 16, with a valid pre-sale code.

The tickets can be purchased on Givēon's website or through Live Nation and Ticketmaster.

Givēon's upcoming tour will start shortly after the release of his newest album, Give Or Take.
