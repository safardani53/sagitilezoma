BTS Jimin Became The New Brand Ambassador For Tiffany &amp; Co

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

Tiffany & Co. has revealed that BTS’s Jimin will be their newest brand representative. Jimin, who was recently appointed Dior’s worldwide envoy, will now be adding another premium brand to his resume. Jimin’s collaboration with Tiffany will commence shortly, with a forthcoming commercial campaign starring the Serendipity vocalist due to debut later this year. As Tiffany & Co.’s newest brand representative, Jimin will play an important role in marketing the brand’s goods and will participate in different advertising campaigns and brand events.

The following is an extract from the formal contract:

“In upcoming ads, Jimin will show off his distinct approach to jewelry dressing by wearing classic Tiffany & Co. designs.”

The company published a sample picture of Jimin wearing Tiffany’s ‘Lock’ bracelet designs, which include half pavé, complete pavé, and diamond embellished styles.

Jimin of BTS is the new Tiffany & Co. brand representative.

With BTS’ Jimin becoming the new Tiffany & Co. brand representative, his ‘It boy’ reputation has certainly been raised. Both Jimin and the company have voiced enthusiasm about their collaboration and are anxious to get started. In his remark, Jimin mentioned:

“It is a pleasure to collaborate with Tiffany and represent the ideals of a company that values design, creativity, and craftsmanship.”

Alexandre Arnault, Tiffany & Co.’s Executive Vice President (Product and Marketing), triumphantly revealed their newest addition to the company in an equally touching message:

“We are excited to welcome versatile talent and performer Jimin of BTS as our newest house representative. He personifies Tiffany and Co.’s vitality, flair, and sense of innovation. We are pleased and ecstatic to reveal our upcoming initiatives with him, which will debut later this spring.”

Jimin of BTS has an obvious worldwide impact, and his collaboration with Tiffany & Co. as their new brand representative is anticipated to enhance the brand’s social image even further. Jimin was also named a worldwide envoy for Dior earlier this year, where his charisma and power were on full show. Jimin’s Dior social media tweets earned a staggering $17 million in EVM, a measure used to calculate the worth of a social media post.

During Paris Fashion Week, he was in charge of 54% of Dior’s total Revenue, demonstrating his influence in the fashion industry. Jimin is anticipated to deliver the same degree of achievement to Tiffany & Co. as he did to Dior. Apart from his ambassadorial duties, BTS’s Jimin is also preparing to start his independent career as a singer. FACE, his debut record, will be published on March 24, with a pre-release song on March 17.
