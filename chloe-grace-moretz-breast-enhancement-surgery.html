Actress Chloe Grace Moretz says being given a push-up bra made her consider breast enhancement surge

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 2, 2024

ACTRESS Chloe Grace Moretz revealed she considered breast enhancement surgery at 16.

The 21-year-old Kick-Ass star said she felt insecure after she was given push-up bras to wear for a film.

She said: “Those little things were insidious. They make you think, ‘Maybe I am unhappy with the size of my breasts’.

"I've had to look at Hollywood my whole life. The people I've been comparing myself to are people who are not real."

Moretz added she has turned down roles that she views as hypersexualised, saying: "I choose the roles that connect to me the most. I'm a very strong woman... I couldn't just be a girlfriend. Oh my god. Literally, no way."

The actress, who will next be seen in new film The Miseducation Of Cameron Post about a teenage girl sent to a Christian-run "gay conversion" camp, did not want to comment on the end of her relationship with David and Victoria Beckham's eldest son Brooklyn.
