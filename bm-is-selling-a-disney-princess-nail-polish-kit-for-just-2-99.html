B&amp;M is selling a Disney princess nail polish kit for just 2.99

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, March 1, 2024

B&M’S latest beauty product is fit for a princess.

The budget retailer is selling a nail kit featuring your favourite Disney characters.

The £2.99 set contains three peelable polishes emblazoned with Belle, Cinderella and Snow White, plus two pink toe separators.

B&M states: “With nails good enough for a princess, what more could you want?

“This nail polish set is perfect for Disney princesses all over the world.”

The price-friendly brand unveiled the kit on its website earlier this week.

But this isn’t the first product to excite shoppers.

We previously revealed how B&M is selling a pair of tan fluffy slippers – and they’re £75 cheaper than the Ugg version.

In related news, B&M is also selling a light-up reindeer for £14.99 - that’s £45 less than the Debenhams version.

While the cut-price store is also selling Disney princess mug and sock sets for £5.99 - and they’re flying off the shelves.
