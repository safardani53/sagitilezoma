Rihannas Gift To Chris Brown &amp; Royalty Shine Bright Like A Diamond Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, September 28, 2024

“Rihanna just wanted to brighten up Chris’ day and do something nice for him and Royalty,” our source told us EXCLUSIVELY. “Last week she sent them both a matching pair of diamond stud earrings. She sent this to his mother’s house instead of his house, because she knew he was out of town and didn’t feel comfortable sending them there because of the recent robbery. She sent a note too, and it read: ‘You and Royalty keep shining bright like a diamond.'”

We’re swooning; that’s just too cute. Rihanna knows that he’s a wonderful father. He’s having a tough time as he fights Royalty’s mother, Nia Guzman, for custody rights, and Rihanna just wanted to be a good friend and do something nice for him! Bonus points for Rihanna? Giving Chris’ daughter a present, too! It truly points to the fact that she cares about him and his entire life. Because let’s face it, Royalty’s his #1 lady!

Let’s talk about how her idea of a fun little gift was two sets of diamond earrings! Girl finally got her money! Rihanna was definitely in a giving mood — and if she was trying to brighten Chris’ day, we’re pretty sure that worked! Chris and Rihanna have a long, deep relationship; Chris gushed in a July 2015 interview about how he has nothing but “love and respect” for “his homie.”

But don’t think that because RiRi’s buying him nice presents, or Chris is expressing his admiration, that the former flames are going to try getting back together.
