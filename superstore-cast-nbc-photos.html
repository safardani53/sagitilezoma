Superstore Cast Photos Of NBCs Workplace Comedy Hollywood Life

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 17, 2024

‘Superstore’ season 5 currently airs Thursdays on NBC. Golden Globe winner America Ferrera stars as Amy (not Emily!) in NBC’s hilarious workplace comedy, ‘Superstore’. The show follows a group of retail workers as they navigate life, love, and everything inbetween, all while stocking the shelves at the big box store, Cloud 9. The series also stars Ben Feldman as Jonah, Lauren Ash as Dina, Nico Santos as Mateo, Colton Dunn as Garrett, Mark McKinney as Glenn, Nichole Bloom as Cheyenne, and Kaliko Kauahi. ‘Superstore’ is one of the best comedies on TV because it’s not afraid to tackle important issues like immigration, unionization, health care, sexuality, workplace sexual harassment, and more. The series was created by Justin Spitzer and has featured amazing guest stars like Chrissy Metz, Cecily Strong, Howie Mandel, Natasha Leggero, Brian Howe, Brenda Song, and more.
