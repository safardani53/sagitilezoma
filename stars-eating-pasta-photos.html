Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

Mamma mia! It’s National Spaghetti Day, so let’s indulge in some carbs and check out the best photos of celebs enjoying Italian food.

Mamma mia! It’s National Spaghetti Day on Jan. 4 which means it’s time to honor one of our favorite noodles. While some are cutting calories at the start of the year, we just can’t resist a holiday, so here we go!

There are so many ways to enjoy pasta, be it our beloved, classic spaghetti, or a spicy pomodoro or rich carbonara. And celebs certainly know the right dishes when it’s time to indulge.

Stars love hitting up top restaurants for a pasta dinner, even if it means a cheat day for their diets. And if New York or LA’s offerings don’t impress them, it’s easy for the celeb set to jet across the pond for a bite in good old Italia.

It’s such a treat to see stars enjoying pasta like the rest of us! Check out all the best photos of celebrities and Italian food in our gallery here.
