Paul Vincent Biography

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

Paul Vincent is the fraternal twin brother of renowned actor Vin Diesel who is known for his role as Dominic Toretto in film series. He is not as famous as his brother and rarely appears on the headlines for his personal and professional career.

Paul Vincent's Early Life

Paul was born in Alameda County, California, on July 18, 1967, with his twin brother, Mark Sinclair (Vin Diesel). He was to mother Delora Sherleen, who is an astrologer, however, he never met his biological father. He belongs to American nationality and African-American ethnicity according to his biological father.

Paul was raised alongside his siblings, Vin, and Samantha Vincent, his half-sister by his mother Delora and step-father, Irving H. Vincent.

Paul Vincent's Career

Paul Vincent has kept himself away from the spotlight throughout his life. He has not revealed any information about his profession. As a twin brother of a talented actor, fans want to know if Paul bears some acting skills. But sadly, he hasn't appeared on any big screen yet.

However, Paul has worked as a sound editor in Vin's short drama film . He was also seen on the set of in a picture alongside now late actor Paul Walker.

Since media has come to know about Paul, many have speculated if he might appear in movies in near future.

Paul Vincent's Personal Life

Paul has always been secretive about his personal life and doesn't want anyone to know about it. He has not disclosed about his marital life however, Vin took to Instagram wishing his twin brother happy birthday with a caption 'You've grown to become a wonderful dad and family man.', which concludes that Paul is married.

Moreover, there is no information regarding his family, children, wife, girlfriend, and past affairs. Paul is definitely living a happy life with his family keeping his personal life under wraps.

Rumors of Vin Diesel's twin brother broke in early when some sources found about his brother. After being questioned by many, Vin finally posted a photo of his real twin.
