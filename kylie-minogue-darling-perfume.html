Kylie Minogue's Darling is back: Here's where you can buy it

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 11, 2024

KYLIE Minogue has finally relaunched her signature fragrance, Darling.

The Spinning Around hitmaker first launched the floral scent back in 2006, and now 15 years later, it's returning to our shelves.

Kylie Minogue's Darling perfume will be available to buy exclusively at The Perfume Shop for £22 for a 30ml bottle or £38 for a 75ml.

  • Kylie Minogue's Darling Eau de Parfum, £17.99 from The Perfume Shop - buy here

If that wasn't enough, you will be even more pleased to learn it is on sale too, and reduced to just £17.99.

Kylie Minogue's Darling perfume s available to buy online and in store at The Perfume Shop, but is now on sale for £17.99.

It's available to buy at The Perfume Shop exclusively now, and will be available to buy across UK and Europe in January 2022.

Darling proved to be a huge hit when it first dropped, and for the sceptics out there, you will be pleased to learn the original scent has remained the same.

It boasts top notes of tropical fruits and florals, including freesia and lychee, passionfruit as well as lily, while it also carries deeper notes of sandalwood , amber wood and vanilla.

Darling features a new design sees the bottle and packing reimagined through a modern lens with a sleek half-moon shaped bottle, and lace-textured lid.

Speaking about the new drop, Scent Beauty Inc.’s CEO, Stephen Mormoris said: "The original launch of Darling was a momentous occasion in the fragrance world. Kylie connected deeply with her fans and customers and it was a global success.

"This time around is no different, Kylie is a timeless beauty, sharing a timeless scent. We are thrilled to be re-releasing this classic with a modern twist into the market."

Kylie's Darling is available to buy in two sizes, 30 ml and 75ml, both are under £40, which we think is an absolute bargain.
