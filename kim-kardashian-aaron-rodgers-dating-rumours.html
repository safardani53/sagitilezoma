Are Kim Kardashian and Aaron Rodgers dating?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, July 17, 2024

KIM Kardashian and Aaron Rodgers are well-known public figures who have established themselves in their respective fields over the years.

A snap of the two together have sparked rumors of a romantic relationship.

Who is Aaron Rodgers?

Aaron Charles Rodgers is an American football quarterback for the New York Jets in the National Football League (NFL).

Rodgers, 39, began his college football career at Butte College in 2002 before transferring to University of California, Berkeley.

The future hall-of-famer set a number of career passing records, including lowest single-season and career interception rates.

Rodgers was selected in the first round of the 2005 NFL Draft by the Green Bay Packers and played for the Packers from 2005 to 2022.

The legend is unanimously recognized as one of the best quarterbacks of all time.

Are Kim Kardashian and Aaron Rodgers dating?

Fans have been speculating on the internet over a rumored relationship between Rodgers and Kim Kardashian.

Swirling speculation started when the two were snapped together in an elevator at a New York Fashion Week party.

However, it has not been confirmed whether or not the two are officially dating.
