I compared Aldi's Toblerone dupe and the original and was stunned at the results

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, January 1, 0001

THEY'RE known for their amazing dupes.

And it's not just versions of designer beauty and make-up products that Aldi customers love, but also their take on popular food items.

One man hit the local supermarket to grab a Milk Chocolate Mountain Bar, which he tried alongside a Toblerone to see if he could tell the difference.

"Right guys I’m just in Aldi and look what they’ve got," Mark said in a video on his Right Guys Review TikTok page.

"They’ve got Aldi Toblerone.

"It’s called Swiss Collection Chocolate mountain bar.

"Let’s get one - let’s try it and see if it’s like Toblerone."

He also grabbed a £1 Toblerone, and couldn't wait to get home to try them both.

Opening the Aldi bar, it looked much the same, with the triangular segments of chocolate.

He broke one off, by pushing the point of the triangle towards the next triangle - snapping it off with ease.

Popping it into his mouth, he gave it a good chew, before declaring: "It’s just a Toblerone!"

Mark then showed how the chocolate segments from Aldi's bar and the Toblerone looked next to one another.

"And there you go, side by side, just the same," he said.

People were quick to comment on Mark's video, with one writing: "Probably the same item.

"Someone who works in a Lidl says Lidl choc seashells are made at the same place guylian are - maybe this is the same."

"I prefer the Aldi one," another wrote.

"Haha its just a Toblerone," someone else commented.

"Only difference is Toblerone is made in Switzerland and aldi is not," another clarified.

But not everyone was convinced.

"Definitely not just like a toblerone. It tastes like cardboard," one wrote.
