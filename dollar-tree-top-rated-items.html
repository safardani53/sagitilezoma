Three Dollar Tree products rated five stars by customers who say it's the 'perfect size'

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 22, 2024

SHOPPERS are praising several Dollar Tree items, and the “perfect size” treats are priced as low as $1.25.

Dollar Tree has long been a go-to spot for affordably priced groceries and home goods.

But, not all items receive the same level of enthusiasm from shoppers.

Certain products have achieved consistent five star ratings at the retailer, and they are quick and easy additions to any grocery list.

The U.S. Sun compiled a full list of the chain’s top tier products, as rated by customers.

1. NISSIN BEEF RAMEN

The Nissin Top Ramen Beef-Flavored Noodle Soup comes in a five count pack for just $1.25.

This essentially brings the cost of five meals to a jaw dropping low of just 25 cents.

But even more encouraging than the low price is just how positive shoppers are in the reviews for the simple but delicious dish.

One customer called the ramen a beloved “family favorite.”

“Cheap and good. With shortages and inflation around, it's nice to know that ramen is still available at a good price,” they wrote on Dollar Tree’s site.

Another called the flavor “the very best.”

“My daughter loves this food product,” the shopper said. “She is always telling me to buy this at your store. They are the best, would not buy them anywhere else.”

2. CARAMEL COW TALES MINIS

Another treat you should add to your Dollar Tree basket are the mini Caramel Cow Tales.

Each chewy caramel candy comes with a cream center, and at 140 calories a serving, it can make a sweet addition to any diet.

The candy dessert can also make a great gift for any occasion.

“Awesome Christmas gift,” one shopper said in a review. “Ordered a case and my in laws loved it.”

Another customer was amazed with the low price of just $1.25 for four ounces of gooey caramel sweetness.

“This is a family favorite and the price per pound is almost half what I could find anywhere online,” they wrote.

3. HUNGRY JACK COMPLETE ORIGINAL PANCAKE MIX

Another five-star rated item at your local Dollar Tree is the Hungry Jack Complete Original Pancake Mix.

Breakfast lovers are constantly on the lookout for a high quality pancake option, and luckily, Dollar Tree has one on sale for just $1.25.

With just a little vegetable oil and a skillet, this mix will supply you with enough pancakes to feed a whole classroom in no time.

With 12 servings per container, shoppers are convinced it’s one of the best pancake mix deals on the market.

“We are purchasing for our food bank and this is the perfect size and price for that purpose,” one customer said in a review.

HOW TO SAVE MORE AT DOLLAR TREE

With most items priced at just $1.25, you’re already saving a ton when you buy at Dollar Tree.

But there are ways to boost your savings even more when shopping at the bargain retailer.

For one, consider purchasing your items in bulk, so you’ll be less likely to spend more at a different store when you run out of an essential.

Buying in bulk online can also sometimes get you deals like up to 50 cents per unit.

If you choose to ship your online order to a local store, you’ll also save in shipping costs because those shipments are entirely free.

Just make sure to collect your items within six days from the receipt of your pickup email confirmation. 

Additionally, the timing of your shopping trip matters.

Dollar Tree typically receives shipments on Tuesday or Wednesday, so you’ll want to do your shopping trip the day after to locate the best products.

Additionally, timing your trip between 10am and 3pm may be the best option, because this is when Dollar Tree is the least busy.
