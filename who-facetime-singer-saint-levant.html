Who is FaceTime singer Saint Levant?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

TRILINGUAL artist, Saint Levant, is known for his contemporary rap music going viral globally.

Fans of the 22-year-old singer are happy to hear he will be embarking on a US tour this fall.

Who is FaceTime singer Saint Levant?

Saint Levant was born Marwan Abdelhamid but is also recognized by other names, such as 'Lover Boy Levant.'

Levant was born in Jerusalem and spent his childhood years living in the Gaza Strip until the civil war broke out in 2007 - forcing his family to move to Jordan, where he spent 10 years of his life.

He is currently living in California and studying International Relations at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

After spending 10 years in Jordan, he moved to California to pursue higher education. He is currently finishing up his Bachelor's in International Relations at the University of California, Santa Barbara.

How many languages does Saint Levant speak?

The Palestinian-Algerian-French-Serbian is a musician and rapper who often fuses his identity into his music.

Levant's songs are a mix of English, French, and Arabic, making them easily recognizable around the globe.

"Effortless and seductive trilingual wordplay has established him as a breakout global star," his bio reads. "Inspired by influential Arab thinkers from generations past and contemporary artists alike, Saint Levant’s music is nostalgic towards the early 2000’s, yet intensely forward thinking, surpassing the limits of genre."

Apart from music, Levant is also very involved with giving back to his community. His 2048 Fellowship helps ground-breaking Palestinian artists by covering their living expenses with a one-year stipend.

When is Saint Levant going on tour?

Levant will begin his From Gaza, With Love Tour in Washington, DC, on October 23, 2023.

He is expected to visit cities like New York City, Chicago, San Francisco, and Los Angeles.

Tickets are available for sale on his official website and Ticketmaster.

Levant has over 1.9million monthly listeners on Spotify with his most streamed song on the app being his single, Very Few Friends.
