Charlotte Crosby screams in agony as she breaks nose in painful swimming pool accident

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, May 10, 2024

CHARLOTTE Crosby screamed in agony after she broke her nose in a painful swimming pool accident.

The former Geordie Shore star was left covered in blood after the terrible incident on holiday in Cape Town, South Africa.

Fans were left horrified when the moment the accident occurred aired on her reality show, The Charlotte Show, last night.

Charlotte, 28, was pulled into the pool by her friend Lauren, but as the star was unprepared, she toppled in on top of her, bashing her face off the top of Lauren's head.

As she resurfaced from the water, Charlotte immediately started screaming and crying, waving her hand in front of her face to tell her pals and boyfriend Joshua something was wrong.

Speaking to the camera, Lauren said: "Charlotte's nose has just bashed off my head.

"I just hope it's not as bad as that crack sounded."

Back in the pool, Charlotte cried out that it hurt leading Joshua to jump in and grab her and walk her out of the water.

As she climbed the steps, blood could be seen running from her nose.

She cried: "I'm going to faint."

Speaking to the camera, Joshua said: "She's hit her face hard. This looks bad."

Charlotte first alerted her fans to her injury in November, sharing a picture of herself lying in the back of an ambulance.

With dried blood under her nose, she captioned the shot on Instagram: "It's all fun and games until you break ya nose."

A rep for Charlotte revealed at the time: "Whilst filming the Charlotte slipped on the side of her hotel pool and banged her nose fracturing it in three places.

"Charlotte has now been discharged from hospital she is in good spirits and will see a specialist on her return to the UK."
