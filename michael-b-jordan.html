Michael B. Jordan Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Michael was raised in Santa Ana, California for about two years. At the age of , he moved to Newark, New Jersey with his parents. There he enrolled at Newark Arts High School. He was active in his high school playing basketball.

He appeared as a model for local companies, Modell's sporting goods, and Toys "R" Us several times before he began his acting career. He made his debut in the entertainment industry in 1999.

In that year, he appeared in an American crime drama television series The Sopranos, and an American film Black and White. In he played the role of Wallace in an American crime drama television series The Wire; he was well appreciated for this role.
