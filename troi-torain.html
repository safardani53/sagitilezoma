Who is Troi Torain:

Troi Torain is a famous Radio Host. He was born on May 3, 1964 and his birthplace is Scotch Plains, NJ. Troi is also well known as, Broadcast radio personality and DJ who rose to fame under the professional moniker Star. He is best known for having hosted the daily Power 105.1 show Star & Buc Wild and the podcast Star In The Morning. 

Troi is originated from United States. After being let go from Power 105.1 he was replaced by Big Tigger.
