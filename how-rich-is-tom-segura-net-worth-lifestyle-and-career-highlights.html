How Rich Is Tom Segura? Net Worth, Lifestyle And Career Highlights

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, April 28, 2024

Tom Segura has a net worth of $12 million as an American stand-up comedian, actor, and podcaster. He is a well-known figure in the world of stand-up comedy. Tom Segura is well-known not just for his comedic abilities, but also for his Netflix specials and podcasts. Aside from his incredible skill, the comic sometimes causes controversy with his outrageous quips.

Tom Segura’s Net Worth

Tom Segura’s net worth is $12 million as of August 2023. According to accounts, he earns between $1,250 and $2,500 every week. His sources of revenue include live events, movies, television series, podcasts, and YouTube channels. Segura also receives money through advertising, brand sponsorships, and product sales.

Lifestyle

Tom is careful about his physical appearance and fitness. As a result, he adopts an active lifestyle and consumes nutritious meals. Segura’s daily workout includes jogging, lifting, squats, push-ups, runs, and deadlifts. He also eats in tiny quantities and restricts his sugar and carbohydrate consumption. The Netflix actress is wearing a Rolex GMT-Master II in 40mm, which costs $15,000.

Personal Life

Tom Segura was born in Cincinnati, Ohio, and has lived all throughout the United States. Tom Segura is married to Christina Pazsitzky, sometimes known as Christina P. Pazsitzky a stand-up comedian, podcaster, writer, presenter, and TV personality who was born in Canada. In Los Angeles, California, the comedy couple met when he was 23 and she was 26. The Los Angeles-based actress has also resided in Minneapolis, Minnesota, Milwaukee, Wisconsin, and Vero Beach, Florida.

Tom Segura’s Professional Career

The stand-up comedian from Los Angeles started his career immediately after graduating from Lenoir-Rhyne University. For the first few years, he worked during the day and performed stand-up comedy in the evenings. The comedian interned at Kopelson Entertainment. He also had his first paid job as a logger. He specialized in producing transcripts of reality programs like Extreme Makeover and My Big Fat Obnoxious Boss. In 2007, Tom Segura professionally launched his standup comedy career by appearing at a number of festivals. The multi-talented actor has appeared in seven films. As a consequence, he has garnered not only fame but also money. The Netflix star made his acting and writing debut in the 2010 film ‘Frank Advice.’

He played the part of Mexican-American businessman Dan Pena in the film. Segura also performed in and directed the 2013 short film ‘9 Inches.’ Similarly, he appeared in the 2016 film ‘The People’s Mayor.’ In the 2017 film ‘I Need You To Kill,’ he also portrayed himself. Segura appeared in the comedy-drama film ‘Instant Family’ in 2018, with Mark Wahlberg and Rose Byrne. Later that year, Segura was cast in the supernatural horror thriller ‘Countdown.’ Finally, he played a policeman in the 2020 comedy film ‘The Opening Act.’

Tom Segura made his television debut in the 2008 comedy Gary Unmarried. Later, he appeared in Happy Endings, another ABC comedy. Similarly, he appeared in Workaholics, a Comedy Central comedy that aired in 2011. Segura appeared on the program This Is Not Happening in 2015 when he revealed his real-life experiences. Furthermore, the stand-up comic has been on Atom TV, Tom Segura: Mostly Stories, and other programs. In addition, Segura and his wife signed a TV contract with CBS in 2018 for their program ‘The Little Things.’ The Ball Hog comedian delivers live events in places all over the globe. The Netflix sensation travels the globe, with the majority of his gigs selling out. Tom Segura is currently on tour with his ‘I’m Coming Everywhere World Tour.’
