Who is Nick Cannons ex-girlfriend, Brittany Bell?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, February 29, 2024

SINCE divorcing Mariah Carey, Nick Cannon's love life has been riddled with many other ladies.

One of those women happens to be Brittany Bell, who Nick shares a number of children with.

Who is Brittany Bell? 

Brittany Bell, 34, is a former NBA dancer for the Phoenix Suns, model, and pageant title holder.

She hails from Barrigada, Guam, and was a first-generation college graduate from the Walter Cronkite School of Journalism and Mass Communications.

Brittany won the Miss Arizona USA 2010 contest and represented Arizona in Miss USA 2010. 

She did not advance beyond the opening round of the competition and was eliminated from the pageant.

Eventually, Brittany went on to win Miss Guam 2014 and moved on to represent Guam in Miss Universe 2014.

However, she failed to place in the competition and was sent home. 

When did she date Nick Cannon? 

Nick was married to Mariah Carey from 2008 to 2014.

He reportedly met Brittany in 2015.

The two were on and off for a while and she gave birth to their son while they were supposedly “on a break” back in 2017.

The pair reportedly split for good but were spotted on November 9, 2020, holding hands leaving a restaurant in Malibu, California, after celebrating her 33rd birthday.

In December 2020, the former couple welcomed their second child together: Powerful Queen Cannon.

On September 30, 2022, the two became parents for a third time to Nick's 10th child Rise Messiah Cannon.

Do they have kids together?

Nick and Brittany have three children together, Golden "Sagon" Cannon, Powerful Queen Cannon, and Rise Messiah Cannon.

They announced the birth of their baby girl on Instagram.

"The best gift ever, we have been surprised with A GIRL!!!!!" Brittany wrote on Instagram with pictures of her and Cannon with their children.

He is also a father to fraternal twins, Monroe, 9, and Moroccan Scott, 9, from his marriage to Mariah. 

Nick told ABC News in 2016 that his children brought joy into his life saying he wasn't against having more.

He said: “I'm not against it. I talk about that all the time. I love children. Everybody knows that."

In 2021, Nick welcomed three new children with two different women.

He and Abby De La Rosa welcomed twins Zion Mixolydian and Zillion Heir in June 2021, just days before he and model Alyssa Scott welcomed their child, Zen, together.

Zen tragically lost his life at five months old after battling a brain tumor, according to Nick.

He shared the news with the audience of his talk show on Tuesday, December 7, 2021.

In August 2022, Nick posted a maternity video of Brittany Bell confirming that baby number 10 is on the way.

A month later on Friday, September 30, Nick and Brittany revealed they welcomed their third child together.

The Masked Singer host shared with his Instagram followers: "Another Blessing!!! As my journey on this planet becomes more and more remarkable and unfathomable, all I can do is thank God and continue to ask the Most High to order my steps.

"He has given me stewardship and dominion over a family dynamic that to some is unimaginable.

"But more importantly he has blessed me with loving individuals to guide me with care through this purposeful life..."

"48 hours of excruciating pain and life risking danger to welcome 10 pounds of Love and joy named RISE MESSIAH CANNON."

On November 3, 2022, rumors swirled that he was expecting baby number 12, his second with model Alyssa Scott.

Who are Nick Cannon’s exes? 

Nick dated model Jessica White during the summer of 2020.

The two split in August just a few days after Brittany posted a photo of her baby bump on social media.

Jessica did not explain as to why they split but alluded to “not being valued” in an Instagram post. 

She shared a comment on her Instagram saying: “Someone who values you, wouldn’t ever put themselves in a position to lose you.

“I’m so blessed, I’m whole, and ready to embrace a single life free from all baggage, I wish nothing but the best for @nickcannon.".

Jessica joked about being single now and wanting to date Brad Pitt writing: “But truly excited for the next chapter in my life.
