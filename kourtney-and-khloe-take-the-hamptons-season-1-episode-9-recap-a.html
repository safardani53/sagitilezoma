Kourtney and Khloe Take the Hamptons Season 1 Episode 9 Recap: A House Divided

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, July 12, 2024

On Kourtney and Khloe Take the Hamptons Season 1 Episode 9, Scott Disick was forced, finally, to answer the question of what to do with his parents’ house.

If you watch Kourtney and Khloe Take the Hamptons online, or know anything about Scott Disick, you know this was bound to be a traumatic experience.

Not only because his parents passed away recently and how tragic that admittedly is, but because he handles weighty issues as poorly as any grown man alive.

Watch Kourtney & Khloe Take the Hamptons Season 1 Episode 9 Online

The elephant in the room for much of the season, his parents’ place became the focal point of Kourtney and Khloe Take the Hamptons Season 1 Episode 9.

Not because he took the initiative, obviously. That was all Kourtney Kardashian, who took it upon her pregnant self to clean the place out and fix it up.

Her idea was that perhaps they would keep it as a second home. Scott is receptive to this idea, but still won’t go over there or talk seriously about it.

Kourtney also believes the Eastport, N.Y., residence could be a great test of her renovating and decorating skills, a second (third? 12th) career she aspires to.

Scott tells her to go for it in that regard, too, but when she starts bringing back boxes of stuff of is, he gets more and more spooked and puts the walls up.

Kourtney wants him to go through and hold onto old items and photos, including one that shows how son Mason looks exactly like Disick did as a kid.

All it does is create more anxiety for him, though, driving home the idea that his deceased parents aren’t around anymore and making him want to flee.

Soon enough, he’s on the phone with a real estate agent telling them to unload it as fast as possible. Kourtney feels like a failure, but realizes it isn’t personal.

The B story of the night involved the Dash store in the Hamptons and how the staff is becoming friends with Khloe, which Kourtney does not approve of.

We’re really running low on story material – French Montana and Khloe Kardashian in bed could only fill 30 seconds this week – with this whole charade.

Bottom line: Khloe wants to be nice and fraternize with the help, Kourtney draws a harder line, and Jonathan Cheban was also there sans Kim Kardashian.

In the end, they meet in the middle. Kourtney says she’s not going to change her management style, but she doesn’t have to be a ruthless troll either.

Words to live by.
