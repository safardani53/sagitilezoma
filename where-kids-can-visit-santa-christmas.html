Here's where the kids can visit Santa this Christmas - with free toys, workshops and treats for pets

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, July 20, 2024

MEETING Santa is the highlight of many family’s first Christmas', and John Lewis has got it down to a fine art.

Whether you're looking to treat the kids, old or young or even one very lucky, festive pooch - it's all on offer.

It's the most magical time of the year, and John Lewis want to spread yuletide joy with their exceptional Meet Santa experiences.

Available to book at johnlewis.com, if you're feeling festive, or want to get in the mood; we have everything you need to know about Meet Santa at John Lewis here.

They have created a number of unique Meet Santa experiences across a number of locations; and there are options for all the family.

Held in all John Lewis locations throughout the country, Meet Santa

Experiences are accessible to all families nationwide.

Each experience is different based on location, and is priced £15 - 20 per child, or per dog (yes, you read right) with every extra adult costing £5.

All children will receive a free LEGO(R) toy worth £10, and there's even the option for quiet times, if that's more comfortable for your family.

It already looks better than ever? But what does that entail?

Meet Santa

What could be more festive than meeting the big man himself?

John Lewis has brought back their popular Meet Santa experience, with fun for all the family.

Children young and old will be able to meet Santa, and receive a free LEGO(R) gift.

Plus, a professional photo will be taken with the option to buy for memories that will last a lifetime.

Lasting about five minutes (with an additional ten if you buy the photo) it'll be on offer in Cheadle, Cribbs Causeway, Kingston, Southampton, Horsham and Liverpool.

Looking for something a little different? The kids (and dogs) can pull up a chair and enjoy tea and cake with Santa who will also be giving out gifts.

It'll last approximately 40 minutes and will be located in the Place to Eat locations in Bluewater, Brent Cross, Cambridge, Canary Wharf,

Cardiff, Chelmsford, Cheltenham, Chichester, Edinburgh, Exeter, Glasgow, High Wycombe, Leeds, Leicester, Milton Keynes, Newcastle, Norwich, Nottingham, Oxford, Poole, Reading, Solihull, Stratford City, Trafford and White City, and Ipswich.

Santa Paws

By now, you've heard us mention treats for your four legged friends too?

Well good news for the pets; furry friends are welcome this Christmas at John Lewis.

Dogs are always welcome at all John Lewis stores, so Christmas is no exception - and at no extra cost.

Santa Grotto & The Magic Tree

Taking place in Peter Jones, Welwyn, Oxford Street there's a very special surprise in store.

Kids can discover Santa's Magic Wonderland, plus get involved with Magic Tree Workshops; planting the seed and decorating your tree with help from Santa's elves.

Each child will receive your very own living mini Christmas tree and a gift from Father Christmas - with the opportunity to buy a personalised photo with Father Christmas.

And if the above wasn't enough, each child will also receive a gift from Santa.

John Lewis have partnered with LEGO to make sure each child will receive a LEGO® or LEGO DUPLO® gift worth £10 - ensuring it's a present they really want.

Plus, John Lewis is helping all children can visit Santa in comfort, with a dedicated quiet (SEN) time, taking place during the first hour of every Sunday for children with additional needs.

If you have any other further requirements than John Lewis recommend to get in touch so they can accommodate at every store and each child can meet Santa.

So if this sounds like your kind of thing? You're in luck, you can find out more information and how to book at johnlewis.com here.
