Who is Taylor Swift's father, Scott?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, August 9, 2024

SUPERSTAR Taylor Swift seems to have a close-knit relationship with her parents.

Now, fans want to know more about the music icon's family, particularly her father, Scott Swift.

Who is Taylor Swift's father, Scott?

Scott Kingsley Swift, 71, was born in Bryn Mawr, Pennsylvania, on March 5, 1952, to Archie Dean and Rose Baldi Swift.

After becoming a stockbroker, he built a career as a financial advisor for The Swift Group under Merrill Lynch, per People.

He and his wife, Andrea Swift, raised Taylor Swift and her brother, Austin, on a 15-acre Christmas tree farm in Wyomissing, Pennsylvania.

In 2003, Scott moved his financial business to the Swift family's new location after purchasing a home in Hendersonville, Tennessee so Taylor could pursue her country music dreams in nearby Nashville.

He has since continued to support his daughter's massively successful career.

As People reported, Taylor dedicated her 2008 Grammy wins to Scott and thanked both of her parents in her speech, saying: "This is for all those times that you said I could do whatever I wanted in life.

"And my mom, you're my best friend."

Scott and Andrea have both attended multiple shows on their daughter's record-breaking Eras Tour.

Per Page Six, Scott gained attention at a November 2023 Argentina show when he danced and celebrated with Travis Kelce after Taylor made a lyric change to her song Karma in honor of her NFL star boyfriend.

The lyrics read: "Karma is the guy on the screen, coming straight home to me."

The pop sensation instead sang: "Karma is the guy on the Chiefs, coming straight home to me."

How long have Scott and Andrea Swift been married?

Scott and Andrea have been married for over 35 years.

The couple wed in Houston, Texas, on February 20, 1988.

On December 19, 1989, they welcomed their first child, Taylor, in West Reading, Pennsylvania.

According to Nicki Swift, Scott and Andrea split in 2012, after 25 years of marriage.

Who is Taylor Swift's brother, Austin?

Austin Swift was born in West Reading on March 11, 1992.

The 31-year-old is an actor who has appeared in multiple films and TV series.

According to his IMDb page, he was featured in the movies Live by Night, Cover Versions, Braking for Whales, We Summon the Darkness, and I.T.

Austin appeared in the television shows Still the King and Embeds, as well as the music video for his sister's festive holiday song, Christmas Tree Farm.

He is also a producer who has worked on multiple of Taylor's projects, including the Folklore: The Long Pond Studio Sessions documentary and the music video for the track I Bet You Think About Me.
