Who was Manuel Ojeda? | The US Sun

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, May 18, 2024

POPULAR Mexican actor Manuel Ojeda rose to fame in the early 1970s.

On August 11, 2022, it was announced that he passed away at the age of 81.

Who was Manuel Ojeda?

Manuel Ojeda is a TV and film actor who was born on November 4, 1940, in Mexico.

Over the years, he has been seen in several productions, but he is arguably best known for his role as Zolo in Romancing the Stone.

Ojeda was also seen in Fuego en el mar, Salón México, and El infierno de todos tan temido, Corazón guerrero, and Enemigo íntimo.

While Ojeda is arguably one of the most popular actors in Mexico, he often kept his personal life private and out of the media.

What was Manuel Ojeda's cause of death?

Ojeda's death was first announced by Mexico's National Association of Actors.

"The National Association of Actors deeply regrets the death of our partner Manuel Salvador Ojeda 'Manuel Ojeda', a member of our union," they said in a tweet.

Despite being confirmed, a cause of death was not immediately disclosed.

Shortly after the news was announced, several fans took to Twitter to remember the late star.

Among those who paid tribute was Mexican actress Oka Giner, who wrote: "I deeply regret the departure of Mr. Manuel Ojeda... I take many lessons from him in my heart. My deepest condolences to his family. fly high."

Mexican actress Erika Buenfil, added: "Rest in Peace Manuel Ojeda. Great fellow friend, affectionate, gentleman a big hug to his family."
