Snow school closures: Which schools are shut today due to weather?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, February 1, 2024

SCHOOLS are closed in parts of the UK today after snow blanketed the North and Midlands.

Here's all you need to know about the snowfall on February 2.

Are schools shut today over bad weather? 

Yes, schools in the snow affected areas are closed. 

Parts of northern England have seen heavy snowfall overnight, with the Met Office warning rural communities are at risk of becoming cut off and widespread travel disruption is likely.

Schools are shut in much of Yorkshire, Cumbria, and Greater Manchester. Derbyshire has also seen closures.

What should I do if I can't get my kids to school?

During lockdown schools are only open for children of key workers and vulnerable kids.

But if your kids are attending school it's hard to plan for situations that are out of your control when they close.

Try and contact work as early as possible to let them know you have the children for the day.
