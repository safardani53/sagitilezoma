Ryan Gosling Writes Daughter Esmeralda's Name On His Hand: Cute Photo!

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

She’s always with him! Ryan Gosling took his new daddy cuteness to the next level when he wrote his daughter Esmeralda’s name on his hand before heading out in L.A. on Friday, Feb. 27.

The Only God Forgives actor, 34, gave a glimpse of the touching tribute while he was out running errands. While it’s unknown whether it’s a real tattoo, Gosling was photographed with her nickname “Esme” written across his four fingers.

Gosling has been busy filming the upcoming drama The Nice Guys — but did step away to have some fun in Disneyland with director Guillermo del Toro on Feb. 22. While showing off his ink, the hunky star kept it casual in a white T-shirt, black leather jacket, and baseball cap.
