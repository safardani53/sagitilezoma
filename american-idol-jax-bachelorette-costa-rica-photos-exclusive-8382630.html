Musician Jax Celebrates Bachelorette Party in Costa Rica

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Musician Jax had a bachelorette party to remember!

The 27-year-old singer, and TikToker traveled with a group of friends to Costa Rica to celebrate her upcoming wedding to fiancé Braverijah Gregg.

Alaska Airlines provided flights for the party-goers and hosted them in their special lounge prior to boarding. During their tropical getaway, the group stayed at a Stay in Tamarindo vacation rental, where they enjoyed meals prepared by a private chef from Belly Fed Better.

Activities included boat excursions, swimming, sunbathing and exploring the stunning scenery.

Jax announced her engagement on Instagram in January. "We are officially engaged!" she wrote, alongside a video set to audio of her singing her song "Ring Pop."

"I get to marry my best friend. And as much as I love this ring…I will always be ok with a Ring Pop," she added.

The video featured throwback footage of Jax and Gregg — who is a director and photographer — cuddling on the couch as Jax showed off a Ring Pop on her finger. "How the New Year started in our one bedroom apartment in 2021," a text caption read.

The upload then cut to footage of Gregg's surprise New Year's Eve proposal. He could be seen leading Jax into a hotel room decorated with a trail of rose petals and dozens of candles. Jax appeared overcome with emotion when Gregg got down on bended knee and presented her with a gorgeous diamond sparkler.

"Is that a yes? Yes. It's a yes," she wrote over the video.

It was a big night for Jax, as she also performed live in New York City's Times Square during Dick Clark’s New Year’s Rockin’ Eve with Ryan Seacrest broadcast.

Back in April 2022, Jax shared the story of how she and Gregg met — in song form! "I was on a date, and you were on a date," she sang in an Instagram clip. "But when we caught our dates making out with each other, we thought, 'Welp. Great.'" They ended up leaving the party together, and the rest is history.

In June 2022, Jax released the single "Victoria's Secret," which earned the singer her first entry onto the Billboard Hot 100. The song charted at No. 35 and also became gold-certified in the U.S.
