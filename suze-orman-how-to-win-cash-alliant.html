Suze Orman reveals how savers can win $10,000 - but you'll need to be quick

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, May 27, 2024

SUZE Orman is urging individuals to act quickly for a chance to win up to $10,000 by opening an account with Alliant Credit Union.

The producer of her podcast shared a tip from the money guru, reminding listeners to enter the sweepstakes before the deadline on October 13.

“You have to open your account now or you'll miss out on the chance to win $10,000, $5,000, or even $1,000,” Robert said.

In total, Alliant is giving away $20,000 worth of cash. There will be five $1,000 winners and one of each gets $5,000, or $10,000.

To enter, you’ll need to open Ultimate Opportunity savings account with Alliant.

According to an earlier podcast in August, if you had opened an account before September 13, your chances to win doubles.

On the other hand, if you open an account from now until October 13, your name will be entered just once.

Plus, the second and third place winners will be featured on the podcast.

And the grand prize winner will get “a virtual one on one” with Mrs Orman.

Mrs Orman has a partnership with Alliant to offer the sweepstakes.

She has alsoendorsed getting a $100 bonus by opening an Ultimate savings account with Alliant.

To qualify for the reward, you need to open an account and make 12 straight monthly deposits of $100.

Once you do that, you’ll automatically get the $100 bonus.

In addition, the savings account also comes with an interest rate of 0.55%, increasing your cash.

For example, if you put in $100 a month for 12 months, you'd have earned $3.03 in interest alone after a year.

As long as you choose eStatements, you are exempt from fees on the account.

But you might be able to get a higher interest rate if you open up an account with a different firm.

For instance, Quorum offers up a 1% annual percentage yield if you select the 30-month option.

Just be aware that some firms might require a higher initial deposit and possible monthly fees.
