GMA's Amy Robach, T.J. Holmes' Candid Quotes About Each Other

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, September 23, 2024

Amy Robach and T.J. Holmes initially connected after they were tapped to headline GMA3: What You Need to Know in 2020.

“I had done segments over the years with Dr. [Jennifer] Ashton, who I would always seek out to ask for her expertise. We were collaborating almost every day before being on TV together,” Holmes told Resident Magazine in an April 2021 cover story about the duo’s third cohost. “Robach and I have similar backgrounds and a respect for each other’s journeys.”

Robach and Holmes’ relationship status made headlines two years later in November 2022 after photos surfaced of the pair looking cozy in New York City. They were married to Andrew Shue and Marilee Fiebig at the time, though have each asserted they were both going through divorces. Amid the talk show scandal, ABC let them go in January 2023.

Robach and Holmes continued dating, launching a podcast together in December 2023.
