Tallia Storm puts on eye-popping display as she goes braless at TRNSMT

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, June 16, 2024

TALLIA Storm has treated TRSNMT punters to an eye-popping display after going braless in a see-through top.

The Capital Scotland host, 24, posed up for our cameras as she flashed her abs in a white cropped t-shirt and tight leather trousers as she joined in with the second day of festivities at Glasgow Green.

The blonde bombshell teamed her 'rock chic' look with a cropped black leather jacket, silver chunky accessories and a pop of daring red lipstick.

It comes after the Scots singer wowed in a bonkers McDonald's themed outfit on the first day of TRNSMT as she branded it "ketchup chic".

Tallia said this is her first TRNSMT festival and said there is "nothing like it".

The singer told the Scottish Sun: "This is my first TRNSMT. Growing up, I always wanted to go to T in the Park but by the time I was old enough it was over.

"To be here on Glasgow Green celebrating all things Scottish with international names is incredible. There's nothing like it.

"It's fantastic to be able to show people that we have the best crowds.

"The atmosphere is great. I'm here with McDonald's today and we're giving out free fries. I don't think I've ever seen people so excited in my life.

"It's amazing what fries can do. Today it's all about fries before guys, forget the guys.
