Jon Stewart Takes to WWE Ring, Kicks Wrestler in the Gonads

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, October 6, 2024

Well, we now know what Jon Stewart will do after he leaves The Daily Show.

And wrestlers around the WWE should be cowering in fear. Or at least covering their groin.

The Comedy Central host appeared on Monday Night RAW last night, interrupting a segment titled "The Daily Show with Seth Rollins."

It featured grappler Seth Rollins talking trash about Stewart and how he failed as an Oscars host and how he’s a “wuss and a phony" and how no one saw his directorial debut, Rosewater.

Rollins then said he would force Stewart to “retire immediately” if he dared come into the ring because it’s “hard to host a phony news show with your jaw wired shut.”

And then Stewart came into the ring! And he said he has something Rollins will never have: Respect.

And then he kicked Rollins in the nuts! It was awesome!

Stewart has referenced in the past how he watches WWE with his son and Rollins started this feud on February 16 when he told a crowd in Orlando that he could easily take over The Daily Show and make it "watchable."

Stewart responded with a video in which he addressed the wrestler with the following taunt:

“Seth Rollins, you’ve made a lot of mistakes in your life… but this is the biggest mistake you’ve ever made by coming after The Daily Show. You just stepped in a world of hurt my friend."

How right Jon Stewart proved to be.
