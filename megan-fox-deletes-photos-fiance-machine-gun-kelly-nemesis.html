Megan Fox shocks fans as she deletes all photos of fianc Machine Gun Kelly &amp; follows singer's neme

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 13, 2024

MEGAN Fox has shared a cryptic post while seemingly deleting all of her photos with her fiancé Machine Gun Kelly.

Rumors are now swirling that Megan, 36, and MGK, 32, - who got engaged in January 2022 - have ended their relationship.

The famous actress has fueled those split rumors with her most recent Instagram post. 

In the series of sexy photos, Megan teased her cleavage while wearing a tight black dress with steamy cutouts.

After showing off some skin, the star uploaded a short video of a trash bin on fire.

A white letter appeared to be burning up in the crackling flames.

Megan dropped the lyrics of Beyoncé's song Pray You Catch Me in the caption: "You can taste the dishonesty. It's all over your breath."

NO TRACE OF MGK?

For most of their relationship, Megan and MGK appeared hot and heavy in public and on social media.

In the comments section, fans noticed the Emo Girl singer was missing from her social feed and claimed that "she deleted all their photos."

Another person pointed out: "Deleted all photos of MGK and started following Eminem."

A third user explained: "Her and Eminem are good friends. They just haven't spoken to each other a lot since she was with MGK."

The Till Death star appears to be following only three accounts at the moment: singer Harry Styles, actor Timothee Chamalet, and rapper Eminem, 50.

BATTLE RAP

MGK and Eminem have been locked in a war of words since 2012. 

The beef kicked off when MGK commented on how Eminem's daughter, Hailie Jade - who was 16 at the time - seemed "hot."

In a now-deleted tweet, The Dirt actor wrote: "I have to say, she is hot as f**k, in the most respectful way possible cuz Em is king."

In 2015, Eminem referred to MGK's words on his track, Not Alike.

He rapped: "I'm talkin' to you, but you already know who the f**k you are, Kelly.

"I don't use sublime and sure as f**k don't sneak-diss.

"But keep commenting on my daughter Hailie."

MGK would soon release his own diss track, titled Rap Devil, and referenced Hailie's father in his lyrics.

He sang: "I'm doing the same s**t you did back in your day. 

"Life is still real on my side, and I had to take time from the grind to defend myself from someone I called an idol."

ON THE ROCKS?

In May 2022, a source close to Megan and MGK exclusively told The U.S. Sun: "Friends are worried they could be on the rocks as they have been fighting a lot, and getting on each other's nerves. 

"They had an amazing relationship for the first year, but now Megan often feels she is dealing with a grown-up child. "

Megan and MGK met in March 2020 while filming Midnight in the Switchgrass, soon after Megan separated from her ex Brian Austin Green.
