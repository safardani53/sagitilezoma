TODAY's Savannah Guthrie has risked a wardrobe malfunction while wearing a see-through top at a New York City gala.

Savannah attended the Project Healthy Minds' Inaugural Gala for World Mental Health Day in the city on a recent outing.

She opted for a much more risqué top than she usually wears on air.

The 51-year-old donned a see-through transparent top with bling detailing and black accents.

If she had moved in a particular way, she could have risked exposing herself – though she was likely wearing a camisole or something similar beneath the long-sleeved shirt to avoid that.

She wore the transparent top beneath a black leather jacket, paired with a long black skirt.
