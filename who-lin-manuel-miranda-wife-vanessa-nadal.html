Who is Lin-Manuel Miranda's wife Vanessa Nadal?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 26, 2024

LIN-Manuel Miranda and his wife Vanessa Nadal met in high school years before they tied the knot.

They are both at the peak of their careers in their respective fields of law and entertainment.

Who is Lin-Manuel Miranda's wife Vanessa Nadal?

Vanessa Nadal was born in New York, New York.

Vanessa was previously a research and development specialist at Johnson & Johnson after graduating from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 2004.

In 2007, she left Johnson & Johnson to study law at Fordham University.

In 2019, Vanessa became an adjunct professor at Fordham Law School.

Lin-Manuel and Vanessa Nadal met at Hunter College High School, where Lin-Manuel was her senior, however, they never spoke.

Nevertheless, Lin-Manuel reached out to Vanessa on Facebook after they graduated.

Subsequently, they started dating, and Lin-Manuel eventually proposed to Vanessa in 2009 in Madrid, Spain.

They later tied the knot in New York on September 5, 2010.

In October 2017, he posted a picture of them which was taken on the day of the proposal in 2009 on Twitter, with the caption: "The missus & I got engaged in Madrid the day before Halloween 2009, I took this on Photobooth while she called her parents."

They have two sons together - Sebastian and Francisco.

Who is Lin-Manuel Miranda?

Lin-Manuel Miranda was born on January 16, 1980, in New York, New York.

Lin-Manuel is a famous actor, playwright, singer, songwriter, and filmmaker.

He is widely recognized as the creator of the Broadway musicals In the Heights and Hamilton.

He is also the composer of the soundtrack of Disney's Encanto.

As a result of his versatile talents, he has earned himself two Emmy Awards, three Grammys Awards, and three Tony Awards.

What is Lin-Manuel Miranda's net worth?

As of May 2022, Lin-Manuel Miranda has an estimated net worth of approximately $80million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.
