Megan Fox to Remove Marilyn Monroe Tattoo, Labels Icon &quot;Disturbed, Negative&quot;

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Megan Fox has already feuded with Michael Bay, and she isn’t too happy with Shia LaBeouf. But the actress has saved her latest fury for a surprising target:

Marilyn Monroe.

The 25-year-old tells Italian fashion magazine Amica that she’s in the process of laser removing the tattoo of the legend from her forearm because “she was a negative person, she was disturbed, bipolar. I do not want to attract this kind of negative energy in my life.”

Worst Megan Fox photo ever, we know. Here’s one to make up for it, guys.

It’s true that Monroe died at age 36 of a drug overdose. But Fox nine other tattoos on her body, including a quote in honor of Mickey Rourke, her co-star in Passion Play and not exactly a model citizen himself.

 Fox admits she once had a naval piercing, but she also had it removed.

“I did it when I was 16-years-old because I was a fan of Britney Spears. The only time I tried to imitate someone else. But then I thought it was tacky and so I removed it when I turned 20.”
