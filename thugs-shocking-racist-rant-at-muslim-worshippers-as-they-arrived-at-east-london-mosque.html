Thug's shocking racist rant at Muslim worshippers as they arrived at east London mosque

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 21, 2024

SHOCKING footage has emerged of a man launching a foul-mouthed tirade inside a mosque before lashing out at worshippers with a walking stick.

The middle-aged man - who claimed to be a Christian - was seen verbally abusing a group of Muslim men for more than ten minutes after they refused to let him in for being 'drunk'.

He was filmed mocking the Prophet Mohammed and told members of the gathering to "f*** off and go home" as they attended prayer at Bow Mosque in East London.

Witnesses caught the man on camera threatening to "kill" worshippers as they threw him out and restrained him on the pavement outside.

They grabbed the walking stick and kept him subdued while waiting for police to arrive.

Despite repeated insults, threats and attacks, the men remained calm and said: "Nobody hit him."

The disturbing video was uploaded to Facebook yesterday evening and has been viewed more than 20,000 times.

It shows the man leaning unsteadily on his walking stick in the mosque reception area as he launches into his hate-fuelled rant.

A man accuses him of being drunk and says: "I can smell something."

Slurring his speech, the unwanted visitor replies: "Oh, and you know what I can smell from you? S***. So, f*** off."

The men repeatedly ask him to leave but he refuses, saying he wants to wait for the police.

When they ask him to "sit on the floor", he responds: "Why should I sit? No, I stand as a Christian."

He then ridicules their faith, saying he wants to "pray to Mohammed" and insists he can do what he likes in the mosque because it is an "open house".

As more worshippers arrive - including a small child - the man blasts: "S***. How many more can you f****** get in?"

He complains that "not one person has welcomed me" and says he will tell the police.

Another man replies: "Because you're Islamophobic. Why would we welcome you? Look at the state you're in. You're shouting, you're swearing, you hit people."

The man - wearing a black Armani T-shirt - replies: "See? People like this. You tell me you're all the same, lovely, lovely, lovely. You're liars."

The tense stand-off suddenly erupts when he slaps a man's hand away for filming him with a mobile phone.

He appears to grab the man by the wrist and raises his stick, shouting: "You little f***."

Before he can strike, he is pushed out through the open doorway, before he falls over backside-first onto the pavement.

He swings the extendable walking stick at them, bashing it against the door and raging: "F*** off, you f******."

As they surround him on the ground he appears to slap one of the men in the head before they wrestle the stick away from him.

He starts kicking out and shouts: "I'll kill that c***."

One man tells him: "Just relax yourself, nobody wants to hurt you."

The thug then appears to "bite" him and says: "I'm gonna kill ya. It's about time you f****** realised we're not having this anymore. We don't want it, we're British."

The man replies: "So are we."

An alleged victim of the attack later wrote on Facebook: "This is a difficult time for us so we must remain vigilant."

A Met Police spokesman confirmed a man is currently being held on suspicion of racially aggravated assault.

He said: "We were called to Roman Road at 4.10am yesterday to a report of a man behaving in a threatening way.

"Officers arrested a 57-year-old man on suspicion of racially aggravated actual bodily harm and he remains in custody. There were no reports of any serious injuries."
