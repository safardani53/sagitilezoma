Justin Hartley posts first selfie with girlfriend Sofia Pernas as ex Chrishell Stause rings in New Y

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, January 1, 0001

JUSTIN Hartley shared the first selfie with his girlfriend Sofia Pernas while his ex Chrishell Stause celebrated the New Year with Keo Motsepe.

The former pair tied the knot in 2017 before parting ways in 2019.

The This Is Us actor took to Instagram before the clock struck midnight and posted a black and white photo alongside the Blood & Treasure star.

He captioned it: "Last minutes of 2020 here in LA. Bring on 2021! Happy New Year!"

People were loving the post as they shared their thoughts in the comments section.

One person wrote: "happy new year you two," while another wrote, "Cheers to an adorable couple."

Sofia also confirmed her relationship with Justin, 43, on her own social media platform as she shared a snap of the two and wrote: "Sayonara 2020, it’s been real. Happy New Year from our quarantine den to yours."

Justin first met Sofia, 31, in 2015 while working on the show The Young and the Restless while they played an on-screen couple - Chrishell also appeared on the show

Meanwhile, the Selling Sunset star was having the time of her life with her man as she took to her Instagram Stories to share a clip of the pair.

The video starts off with the couple saying "happy new year!" as Keo kisses the reality star a few times on the head.

Chrishell says: "Should we say anything else?" before Keo responds: "Um...I'm very grateful of you."

Chrishell, 39, then adds: "Don't tell them that! You guys get nothing! Why are they so many haters on here?

She continues: "I love the...the good guys...there's a lot of good people on here."

"You're so cute," Keo says while laughing.

Justin shocked family and friends in November 2019 by filing for divorce from Chrishell, a move which reportedly came out of the blue for the realtor.

The cameras were rolling in November when she later claimed on camera that Justin told her over text message with 45 minutes notice that he had filed.

Speaking to friend Mary Fitzgerald, she said: "We had a fight that morning over the phone… we didn't talk things through, and before we had a chance to figure anything out he filed.

"I found out because he text me that we were filed. 45 minutes later, the world knew."

Breaking down in tears, Chrishell said: "I talked to him right after [the text] because I thought that must be a joke, but that was the end of communication. What do you say after that?"

Soon after, Chrishell also hinted that Justin may have cheated, however, many of his friends and family have defended him, including his own mom.

At the time, sources close to Justin alleged to Us Weekly that "Chrishell was very jealous of lots of women Justin interacted with, even if it was people he solely had strict work relationships with."
