Katie Stevens And Paul DiGiovanni Are Expecting Their First Child

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, June 8, 2024

Katie Stevens and her husband, Paul DiGiovanni, are expecting their first child. On November 9, 2022, the actress announced the news on the red carpet of the Country Music Awards. Stevens chose a periwinkle gown with sequined gold appliqués and transparent half-sleeves to show off her baby bulge. DiGiovanni wore a chocolate brown suit with a dark blue button-up. On the night of the celebration, DiGiovanni was seen embracing his wife’s tummy.

The couple did not share the news on social media. Meghann Fally, Katie’s co-star on The Bold Type, hinted at the pregnancy a few weeks ago with a remark on Katie’s Instagram photo. Fally, who portrayed Sutton Brady in the program, had this to say:

“Mom and Dad, Happy Anniversary!”

Katie Stevens’ spouse is a singer with a $10 million net worth

Paul DiGiovanni is a singer-songwriter best known as the main guitarist and backup vocalist for the band Boys Like Girls since 2005. He owns the apparel company Black Carbon Custom, which offers a large range of shirts. For more than a decade, he has played, traveled, and recorded for the band on Columbia Records. He joined the band in 2005 when bandmate John Keefe called him in to help Martin Johnson with recording. DiGiovanni co-produced Singles You Up with Jordan Davis and has usually worked with record producer Scott Hendricks. He contributed to many Warner Music Nashville albums and composed Justin Moore’s military tribute, The Ones Who Didn’t Return Home.

According to IdolNetworth.com, Paul’s net worth is believed to be approximately $10 million. He also has roughly 24,400 followers on Instagram and 35,500 followers on Twitter.

Timeline of Katie Stevens and Paul DiGiovanni’s relationship

Katie Stevens and Paul DiGiovanni dated for four years before getting engaged in 2018. It’s unclear how they met. Stevens revealed the news on Instagram at the time, along with a photo of her ring. They married in Tennessee in 2019. Stevens told People in an interview that she knew DiGiovanni was her soulmate from the beginning of their relationship.

She stated that their first date was five and a half years before they married, and after a few months of dating, she knew they would marry. Katie Stevens rose to prominence after placing eighth on American Idol’s ninth season. The 29-year-old is most known for playing Karma Ashcroft in the MTV romantic comedy series Faking It. She’s also well-known for her role as Jane Sloan in the Freeform comedy-drama series The Bold Type.
