How To Watch Live NCAA Football Games With CBS All Access

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, June 7, 2024

Get kickoff times for college games streaming this weekend (and beyond!) through CBS All Access or your TV provider.

Tradition matters in college football, and that includes a 20-year tradition of SEC on CBS!

We've got another schedule loaded with highly anticipated Southeastern Conference rivalries and showdowns, culminating with the SEC Championship on Saturday, Dec. 19.

As usual, CBS Sports presents their team of lead college football announcers featuring Brad Nessler and Gary Danielson, along with reporter Jamie Erdahl. Gene Steratore is the rules analyst. The pre-game, halftime, and post-game studio show, College Football Today, returns with host Adam Zucker alongside analysts Rick Neuheisel and Brian Jones.

How can I watch or stream SEC on CBS live online?
It's easy! Just sign up for CBS All Access, which is currently available online at CBS.com, on mobile devices via the CBS app for iOS and Android, and on Roku Players, Apple TV, Chromecast, Android TV, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, Fire TV, Samsung Smart TVs, Comcast Flex, Facebook Portal, Vizio Smartcast TVs, LG Smart TVs, Amazon Prime Video Channels, and Apple TV channels.

More college football coverage is also available on CBSSports.com, the CBS Sports app, and CBS Sports HQ, the 24/7 streaming sports news network available to CBS All Access subscribers.

What else can I stream on CBS All Access?
Not only will CBS All Access let you stream SEC on CBS, but sports fans also get loads of other live events. Our sports roster features NFL on CBS (including Super Bowl LV), college basketball (including the NCAA Division I Men's Basketball Championship), elite soccer (including UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, National Women's Soccer League, and UEFA Women's Champions League), professional golf (including the PGA Tour, the Masters, and the PGA Championship), and Hockey East.

You'll also get access to an ever-growing collection of exclusive original series on CBS All Access that you won't find elsewhere. These currently include the critically acclaimed hit The Good Fight, The Twilight Zone, Star Trek: Picard, Tooning Out The News, No Activity, Why Women Kill, The Thomas John Experience, and more.

Of course, you can stream all current CBS Primetime, Late Night, and Daytime shows. Those current primetime series include NCIS, FBI, SEAL Team, All Rise, Young Sheldon, Bob Hearts Abishola, Evil, and The Unicorn, plus upcoming new series like B Positive, The Equalizer, and Clarice. You'll also get every prior season of 14 hit CBS series on demand, including Survivor, NCIS, and Hawaii Five-0

If classics are more your style, CBS All Access offers all of the Star Trek television series, acclaimed hits like Cheers, Perry Mason, and so many others!

The expanding services recently added hit shows from more networks in the ViacomCBS portfolio like BET, Comedy Central, MTV, Nickelodeon, Smithsonian Channel, TV Land, and VH1.
