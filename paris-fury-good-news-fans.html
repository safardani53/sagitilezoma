Paris Fury weeps as she receives really good news - and its all thanks to her fans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 18, 2024

IT may not even be out yet but Paris Fury’s upcoming book has already shot to the top of the Amazon bestsellers list.

The glamorous wife of boxing legend Tyson Fury shared a screenshot of her soon-to-be-released book ranking top on the popular site.

Paris gushed to her Instagram fans: “Wow Amazon bestseller and my books not even out yet.

“I can’t believe the love you guys have shown.

“Thank you all for getting it to number one.”

Paris’ second book, How Does She Do It?: The Kids, Tyson & Me, is due to be released on September 28.

More on Paris Fury

Currently it sits at number one on Amazon’s bestselling books, and shoppers can pre-order it for £11 on hardback or £12.99 on Kindle.

It’s currently ahead of the likes of Richard Osman’s latest The Thursday Murder Club read, The Last Devil To Die, which is ranking fourth.

Meanwhile Colleen Hoover’s It Ends With Us and It Starts With Us popular reads are sixth and seventh respectively.

Paris previously wrote Love and Fury, which revealed “the magical highs and epic lows of life with the Heavyweight Boxing World Champion.”

Most read in Celebrity

Meanwhile, the new book will “reveal the answers to the question she's always asked” such as “how does she manage life as a hands-on mother to six children, as well as supporting world heavyweight husband Tyson, while still looking amazing and finding time for herself?”

Her new book follows the family featuring in a new Netflix show, At Home With the Furys, which was released this month.

The WBC champion and Paris opened up their doors for a behind-the-scenes look at their family life in Morecambe.

Paris and Tyson are parents to Venezuela, 13, Prince John James, 11, Prince Tyson II, seven, Valencia, five, Prince Adonis Amaziah, four, and Athena, two.

The couple have made no secret of their desire to have a big family, with Tyson declaring they want 11 children.

In March, Tyson revealed that he and wife Paris are expecting their seventh child.
