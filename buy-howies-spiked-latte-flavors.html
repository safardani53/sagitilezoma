Learn More About Howie's Spiked Alc-A-Chino Lattes

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, October 3, 2024

HOWIE'S Spiked Alc-A-Chino Lattes is bringing a great combination of coffee and alcohol to a wider audience.

The boozy beverages are perfect for lovers of coffee, cocktails, or both!

How can I buy Howie's Spiked Alc-A-Chino Lattes?

Howie's Spiked Alc-A-Chinos make it easy for consumers to create liquored lattes. Since they're still expanding across the United States, you can check your local spirits shop or liquor store for the adult beverage.

Alc-A-Chino also has a convenient locator on their website so that you can locate the nearest carrier of the spirited drink.

If you don't see them being sold in your area, you can always place an order for them on Get Stocked.

What flavors do the lattes come in?

The lattes are available in many different flavors so everyone can find something they enjoy.

The original brew is a vodka-spiked iced latte made with 100% Arabica coffee, cream, and cane sugar. The mocha latte is also spiked with vodka but also features cocoa imported from Holland.

Coming up next is the soon-to-be-released vanilla bourbon latte which will be made with vanilla from Madagascar and bourbon from Kentucky.

Also waiting in the wings is the dairy-free alternative oat milk latte.

What other brands offer spiked coffee beverages?

Howie's is far from the only brand that can provide you with your hard coffee fix.

Most read in Money

Coffee giant Dunkin' announced its line of spiked iced coffees and teas in August 2023.

Kahlua, known for its coffee liqueur, also sells canned hard coffee.

Twelve 5's Rebel has an impressive lineup of liquored canned coffee flavors. They even offer seasonal holiday flavors like eggnog and cinnamon sugar cookies.
