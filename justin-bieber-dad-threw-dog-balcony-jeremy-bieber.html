Justin Bieber’s Dad Threw Dog Off A Balcony? — Jeremy Bieber Accused Of Abuse

Justin, 20, and Jeremy, 39, have been having problems with Karma ever since they adopted the dog in 2013, according to TMZ. Karma reportedly even bit Justin’s little brother, Jaxon, while living at Jeremy’s home in Canada.

Jeremy hired a trainer for Karma, Trevor Dvernichuk, but apparently that didn’t help much. Trevor was there when Jeremy allegedly threw Karma off the balcony and into a snow bank. He tells TMZ that Jeremy then insisted that Trevor take the dog and keep her until she was properly trained.

Trevor still has Karma, but now Jeremy is claiming that he didn’t abandon the dog, and that in fact, Trevor kidnapped her. The trainer tells TMZ that he’s worried about Karma’a well-being and he may want to involve lawyers, so we’ll have to wait to find out Karma’s fate.
