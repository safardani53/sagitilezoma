All About The Collaboration Between Market x My Chemical Romance

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Market, the renowned streetwear company, has collaborated with My Chemical Romance, one of the most popular emo/pop-punk bands, for a whole new collaboration fashion collection. The capsule collection pays homage to My Chemical Reaction’s trademark style, honoring the band’s nostalgic recollections of the emo/pop punk scenes of the early 2000s.

The collaboration collection consists of a four-piece fashion line that comprises shirts, cardigans, hoodies, and caps. On July 29, 2022, the collection was made available on Market’s official e-commerce site. The series is available for purchase at a retail price ranging from $50 to $170.

More information about the recently announced 4-piece Market x My Chemical Romance clothing line

The capsule pays homage to My Chemical Romance’s classic early 2000s appearance, which included side-swept hair, a red tie, and a black shirt. The collection, which comprises four silhouettes and limited-edition pieces ranging from cardigans to caps, was created in collaboration with Market and Market’s long-time partner, SMILEY. The joint collection honors MCR’s current headline tour as well as the forthcoming When We Were Young festival. The When We Were Young Festival will be held in Las Vegas on October 22, 2022, October 23, 2022, and October 29, 2022.

Malachi Shockley, Market’s head of marketing, stated of the work with MCR, ”

“From the lyrics to their (MCR) clothes, they demonstrated that I could be different while still building a community of like-minded individuals around me.” We wanted to convey the same energy and passion with you via our collection, reminding you that you can shift your attitude and celebrate your whole creative self.”

The band’s ancestors include My Chemical Romance tracks like The Black Parade and Vampires Will Never Hurt You. The collection’s motivation is explained on Market’s official website.

“Capsule delves into MCR’s rebellious attitude and emo image, for which he is recognized globally.”

The Black Parade Cardigan is the collection’s first piece and leads the collaboration capsule. The cardigan costs $170 and is available on Market’s own e-commerce site.

The second item in the collection is the SMILEY MCR Tee, which has an emo smiley that looks like Gerard Way, the band’s main vocalist. The collection’s graphic shirt features Market’s longstanding collaborator, SMILEY. The SMILEY MCR Tee is available for $51 at retail. I Grew Up Listening to My Chemical Romance Hoodie is the collection’s third item. The title typography is highlighted in white-hued block letters above the sweatshirt. The collaboration hoodie is available for $115 at retail.

Then I Grew Up Listening to My Chemical Romance Hat is the collection’s fourth item. The white-colored block letters are embroidered on the front of the hat, which is made of corduroy material. The hat is available for purchase for $51. The commercial picture says, “My Chem Market Part One,” alluding to the collaborative collection receiving additional items. Starting July 29, 2022, the partnership will be available on Market’s official e-commerce site at a retail price range of $50 to $170 in sizes ranging from S to XXL.
