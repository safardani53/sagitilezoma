Major grocery chain brings back 12-day event and shoppers will score 35% off items to 'enhance any h

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

A BELOVED nationwide grocery chain has announced the return of an annual 12-day holiday sales event.

Shoppers will score 35% off a selection of items that will likely be a hit around the dinner table.

Whole Foods Market has bought back its 12 Days of Cheese sales event for 2023.

From December 13 to December 24, customers will be able to score up to 35% of a selection of artisan cheeses.

The selection has been handpicked by the Whole Foods Specialty Cheese team and boasts some of the highest quality and world-renowned cheeses the store has to offer.

The featured cheeses this year include:

  • Sweetgrass Dairy Thomasville Tomme
  • Cypress Grove Humboldt Fog
  • Cowgirl Creamery Mt. TAM Organic Triple Cream
  • Uplands Pleasant Ridge Reserve
  • Vermont Creamery Bijou
  • Neal’s Yard Dairy Appleby’s Cheshire
  • MonS Pyrénées Brébis
  • Papillon Roquefort Revelation
  • Quattro Portoni Quadrello di Bufala
  • Klare Melk Truffle Gouda
  • Emmi Kaltbach Cave Aged Gruyère

Cheese is a popular choice for charcuterie and cheese boards during the festive season.

But artisan and global cheeses can often be expensive

To get the 35% discount, you will need to be an Amazon Prime Member, as Amazon owns Whole Foods.

But if you're not a member, you can score a generous 28% discount anyway.

All customers can also purchase a "12 Days of Cheese" sampler bag, which includes three handpicked cheeses, Savannah Bee Whipped Honey, and crackers for $34.99.

"We've scoured the globe to bring you an array of innovative cheeses at an exceptional value, and our knowledgeable store team is eager to share tips to create the perfect pairings,” said Cathy Strange, ambassador of food culture for Whole Foods in a press release.

“The 12 Days of Cheese sampler bag also offers a quick and thoughtful way to gift a host, enhance any holiday meal or even just treat yourself this December.”

WHOLE FOODS CHRISTMAS

Whole Foods is also helping customers prepare for chriistmas in other ways too.

This year, the store is selling a Christmas Dinner kit for $170 that feeds eight people, which works out to just over $21 a head.

The holiday feast includes a roast turkey, creamy mashed potatoes, green beans, cranberry orange sauce, herb stuffing, and turkey gravy.

But there are also options out there if you are on a tighter budget this year.
