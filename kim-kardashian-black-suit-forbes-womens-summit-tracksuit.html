Kim Kardashian looks sophisticated in a black suit for the Forbes Women's Summit after being picture

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, September 1, 2024

KIM Kardashian stepped out in two very different sartorial selections in New York today.

The reality star, 36, first hit the streets with a pal in the Big Apple in a white vest top and tracksuit bottoms, very much embracing her dressed down look.

However, when the time came for her to make an impression at the Forbes Women's Summit she didn't disappoint, wearing a stunning black suit.
The mother-of-two appeared to go braless in the pinstripe blazer over which her long wavy hair fell.

As she made her way to the event her adoring fans tried their best to get a snap of the star on their phones.

She smiled brightly upon arrival, posing for photographs. The beauty wore a subtle application of make-up befitting of the classy event.

Kim captivated the audience as she spoke on stage with Steve Forbes. She looked a natural, which is no surprise given her status as the queen of reality TV.

What might have surprised fans, however, was Kim's criticism of husband Kanye West's photography skills.

She explained: "I want my Instagram to look a certain way.

"We woke up in the middle of the night and I wanted to take a bunch of pictures.

"Kanye is not the best photographer. It was such a mess. So we scrapped that shoot."

Meanwhile, just yesterday Kim's sister Khloe revealed she could soon become a mum to Kim’s baby – putting her own dreams of parenthood on the back-burner.

The reality star, 32, has made no secret of the fact she wants to have her own children after getting in practice with Kim’s kids Saint and North West.

Yet it seems caring Khloe’s first stab at motherhood could be as a surrogate, after her older sister was told it would be “too dangerous” for her to have another child.

During the season 13 finale of their TV show Keeping Up With The Kardashians, Khloe – who is loved up with new man Tristan Thompson – admitted her life was at a “crossroads” when she was deemed fertile enough to have her own babies.

Initially seeing Kim’s surrogacy request as banter, she replied: “How much money you paying me too get fat, have your child then give it back to you?”

She continued: “I think I should say to my boyfriend ‘I’m pregnant. But it’s not yours, and it’s not mine!

“People think we are so weird already, can you imagine?”

Things took a more serious turn as Kanye West’s wife, 36, insisted her request was genuine and it would mean “everything” to her.
