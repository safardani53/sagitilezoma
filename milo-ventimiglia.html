Milo Ventimiglia Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 8, 2024

Bio:
Milo Ventimiglia (born Milo Anthony Ventimiglia on July 8, 1977 in Anaheim, California) is an American actor. He first rose to fame on Gilmore Girls. He played Rory Gilmore’s love interest from 2001 until 2006. He starred as Peter on NBC’s Heroes for four seasons and as Sean Bennigan on ABC’s The Whispers. Milo notably played Rocky Balboa’s son in Rocky Balboa. He will reprise his role as Jess Mariano on Netflix’s Gilmore Girls: A Year In The Life. Milo now stars alongside Mandy Moore on NBC’s This Is Us.

Best Known For:
Milo is best known for his role as Jess Mariano on Gilmore Girls, Peter Petrelli on Heroes, and Jack on This Is Us.

Personal Life:
Milo dated Gilmore Girls co-star Alexis Bledel from 2002 to around July 2006. He also dated his Heroes co-star Hayden Panettiere from 2007 to 2009.
