Daniel Radcliffe stuns fans with incredible body transformation as Harry Potter star shows off rippe

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, April 10, 2024

HARRY Potter actor Daniel Radcliffe has revealed his rock hard six-pack in the final episode of sitcom Miracle Workers.

The Brit star, 34, stripped down to his tighty whities in the episode and looked incredibly ripped as he tensed and yelled maniacally.

Playing road warrior Sid in the anthology comedy's fourth and final series, Daniel couldn't have been further from the magical schoolboy character of his childhood.

Over four series, Daniel has shed his clothes numerous times but none showed him in such an impressively cut shape.

The jacked actor put his muscles to good use as he ripped robots limb from limb in a spectacularly over the top scene.

His character was on a mission to rid the post-apocalyptic setting of Boomtown of the destructive technological beings, having arrived there from a nuclear ravaged wasteland.

In real life Daniel, who shot to global superstardom thanks to J.K. Rowling's wizarding franchise, couldn't be further from the fearsomely feral Sid.

Daniel became a dad for the first time in April and has been pictured numerous times pushing his son around in a pram.

He is in a relationship with longtime girlfriend Erin Darke, 38, and the doting parents live together in New York.

They met in late 2012 on set of the film Kill Your Darlings.

Daniel previously said: "It'll be a hell of a story to tell our kids one day because of what our characters do with each other.

"Our characters are meeting and flirting with each other, so there is this kind of sweet record of us just meeting for the first time and flirting."

In the film, they have a few romantic scenes together, including a sex scene.

In another interview, Daniel confessed he was immediately attracted to Erin, saying he didn't need to act in their scenes.
