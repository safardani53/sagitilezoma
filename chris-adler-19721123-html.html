Christopher James Adler is an American musician, best known as the founder and longtime drummer of heavy metal band Lamb of God from 1994 to 2019, and a Grammy Award-winning member of Megadeth. Adler was a member of Megadeth from 2015 to 2017, performed percussion on their fifteenth album, Dystopia, and toured several times in support of that album, as well as tours and creative obligations with Lamb. Of God. Adler performed in several local bands including Calibra, Jettison Charlie, Grouser and Darker Days before founding Lamb of God in 1994. Adler is known for his forehand technique, using the heel technique and his unusual approach to the drum kit as a left-handed player on right-handed kits.
