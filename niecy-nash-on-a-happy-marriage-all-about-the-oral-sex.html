Niecy Nash on a Happy Marriage: All About Oral Sex!

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 23, 2024

Niecy Nash knows the key to a happy marriage.

But some women out there may not wanna take the advice this actress has to offer.

Referencing the relationship book she wrote last year (“It’s Hard to Fight Naked”), the former Dancing with the Stars contestant gets very candid in a new interview with Playboy.

“What I say in the book is, ‘A BJ a day keeps the divorce attorney away,'” Nash tells the magazine, explaining:

“I say that because I feel like men are profoundly simplistic, which is the reason I wanted to call my book what I used as the title of chapter three, which is, Stomach Full, Penis Empty: A Woman’s Guide to a Happy Marriage.”
