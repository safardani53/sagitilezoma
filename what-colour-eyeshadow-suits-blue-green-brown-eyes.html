What colour eyeshadow suits green, blue and brown eyes? Your perfect shades revealed

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

IF YOU want your eyeshadow to make the maximum impact, it's important to pick a shade that lets your eyes pop.

No matter what colour your irises, there are plenty of options for the perfect look.

What colour eyeshadow suits blue eyes?

It's suggested that those with brilliant baby blues stick to soft tonal shades like an apricot, coral or Champagne.

To ramp up the impact for a night out bright bronzes and oranges are great for a statement look.

The trick to getting the best orange colour is to track one down with a hint of bronze to enhance the tone of your blue eyes and highlight their gorgeous colour.

Stick to a shade that is more metallic and less yellowy, as that may wash you out and make you look sallow.

What colour eyeshadow suits green eyes?

Smokey eyes can be the perfect way to contrast blazing green eyes.

When it comes to picking out the perfect colour purples and mauves are a surefire win.

If you want drama for a night on the town, try a lighter lavender colour over the whole lid with a wine colour in the crease and under the eye.

You may also want to try reddy tones with the richness helping green eyes to sparkle.

What colour eyeshadow suits brown eyes?

Lucky brown-eyed girls can get away with a huge range of colours.

For the best pop greens are the best option.

Opt for a darker khaki colour and go bold with a smokey eye if you're planning on making a splash for a big night out.
