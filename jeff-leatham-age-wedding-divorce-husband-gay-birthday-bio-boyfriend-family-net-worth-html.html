Jeff Leatham, 46: Stumbling Journey, Husband, Wedding &amp; Quick Divorce

Jeff Leatham, the stylist who was awarded "Chevalier de L'Order des Arts et Letters," had a stammering love life with his gay partner. 

From love at first sight to exchanging the marriage vows, Jeff rode the high waves of married life with Colton Haynes. But, Jeff's romance not only faded but soon ended in a few months after getting married. 

Well, join us to retrace Jeff Leatham's journey of love and divorce, along with a few bio-related details.

The duo ended up sharing the same flight, where they both passed Post-It notes back and forth and also each other's numbers. Soon, the trip turned romantic as they cuddled each other and a day after, Jeff made the romantic gesture of sending flowers to Colton's home quoting, “What movies are made of.”

And the sweetest proposal came in March 2017. 

Jeff, age 46, popped the question under fireworks in Las Ventanas al Paraiso, Mexico and asked Colton, his boyfriend, to exchange the marriage vows with him. He even took his proposal moment to Instagram stating that Colton said "Yes" to the proposal.

Calls Off Seven Months of Married Life

The couple's married life seems pretty good until the rumors came flying showing signs of trouble in their marriage. The married couple made their last public appearance together in March 2018. And the divorce rumors got stronger when the Teen Wolf actor deleted the pair's several pictures from his Instagram.

It became more than obvious after Colton, who enjoys the net worth of $4 million, dropped the word "husband" from his bio, changing his name from Haynes-Leatham back to Haynes.

Finally, the rumors became a reality after Colton filed for divorce on 8 May 2018 in Los Angeles citing irreconcilable differences, cause for their divorce. As per the source, their relationship went off track some time ago, and Colton felt that Jeff pulled him away from his friends and felt like losing himself in the process. The insider stated, 

"Through their relationship, Jeff pulled Colton away from his friends and Colton really started to lose himself."

Colton, who ended their seven months marriage, has even asked the judge to terminate spousal support for both of them and demanded the both to pay their own attorney's fees. 

Well, the seven months married couple seems to have pulled the plug off their relationship.
