Who is Yebba featuring on Drake's new album Certified Lover Boy?

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, September 5, 2024

DRAKE fans are thrilled that he has finally dropped his new album.

Certified Lover Boy is out now and features rising star Yebba.

Who is Yebba and what is her real name?

Abigail Elizabeth Smith, better known by her stage name Yebba, is an American Grammy-award winning singer and songwriter.

The 26-year-old is West Memphis, Arkansas.

She shot to fame when she sang the backing vocals for Chance the Rapper’s Saturday Night Live performance in 2016.

Yebba is co-signed by Ed Sheeran’s record label and released her debut single Evergreen in 2017.

What track has she featured on in Drake's new album Certified Lover Boy?

Yebba sings on Drake's 2021 album Certified Lover Boy.

The track she appears on is called Heartbreak.

Drake dropped the album on September 3, 2021.

Who else has Yebba featured on?

Yebba has collaborated with a number of big artists.

This includes Ed Sheeran, as she is signed to his record label.

She has also worked with Sam Smith, PJ Morton, Mark Ronson and Stormzy.
