Met Gala 2022 red carpet LIVE Kardashian fans say Khloe looks 'uncomfortable' at debut as she say

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, April 27, 2024

THE Met Gala is back in New York City, and numerous stars are walking the red NOW carpet in looks matching the glamorous theme of the night.

Kardashian fans are saying Khloe looks "uncomfortable" as the famous sister said that she's looking forward to having a glass of champagne.

Before arriving to the event, Khloe Kardashian gave an Instagram shoutout to her sister Kim's friend and reality star La La Anthony, who is stunning on the red carpet with a deep red gown and a matching hat.

Kris Jenner also showed her pride for La La by calling her a "sixth daughter" in a red carpet interview with the star, who is co-hosting the Vogue Livestream of the event.

Actress Blake Lively paid "homage" to New York City with her copper gown that transformed into a stunning blue display as it appeared to patinate.

Meanwhile, influencer Emma Chamberlain arrived to the red carpet with a new platinum hairdo.

On April 11, 2022, three weeks ahead of the Met Gala, Vogue announced the official dress code for the event: "gilded glamour, white-tie."

Guests have been asked to embody the Gilded Age New York era, circa 1870 to 1890.

Viewers can stream red carpet coverage on Vogue’s official website, fuboTV, and TikTok.

Read our Met Gala 2022 live blog for the very latest news and updates…

  • Two-part Met Gala

    Co-hosts Vanessa Hudgens and Hamish Bowles explained that this event was the second part of 2022’s Met Gala festivities.

    The gala that occurred earlier this year was making up for the canceled event.

    These galas are two different events that both highlight American fashion.

  • Near wardrobe malfunction

    Nicki Minaj said that the designers made the cup size on her gown too small, causing her some near wardrobe malfunctions on the red carpet as the rapper’s boobs were threatening to spill out of the gown.

    Nicki was wearing a black hat with jewels covering her face and body.

  • Marilyn’s iconic dress

    Kim Kardashian wore Marilyn Monroe’s dress on the red carpet.

    Marilyn wore that exact dress when she sang happy birthday to JFK in 1962.

    Kim said that she lost 16 pounds in order to wear the famous dress.

  • Just married

    Freshly wed couple Brooklyn Beckham and Nicola Peltz took to the red carpet looking monochromatic and in love.

  • Mayoral fashion

    New York City Mayor Eric Adams arrived at the Met Gala red carpet wearing a suit that says “end gun violence” on the back.

  • Blake Lively’s dress transformed

    As Lively walked up the Gala’s iconic steps, her bronze dress was revealed to actually be a light blue gown with a much longer train.

  • Blake Lively’s dress transformation

    The actress arrived to the carpet in a metallic-colored dress, but the dress changed as she walked up the iconic steps.

  • Bold choice

    Kylie Jenner wore a white backward baseball cap with a veil and a white gown.

  • Table settings

    The Gala’s tables were set with Gilded Glamour-esque chairs, silverware and place settings.

  • Who is Tom Ford?

    Designer Tom Ford served as an honorary co-chair of the Gala this year alongside Vogue editor-in-chief Anna Wintour and Adam Mosseri.

    Thomas Carlyle Ford was born on August 27, 1961, in Austin, Texas, but spent most of his childhood in Santa Fe, New Mexico. 

    After graduating from the Parsons School of Design in New York, Ford launched himself into the world of fashion.

    In his earlier days, he worked as a design assistant for Cathy Hardwick for nearly two years.

    A number of top-tier celebrities like Rihanna, Beyoncé, Brad Pitt, and Kim Kardashian have been spotted wearing the Tom Ford brand over the years.

  • Tom Ford’s appearance

    Designer and honorary co-chair Tom Ford arrived on the red carpet wearing a classy suit.

  • Who is Anna Wintour?

    Since 1988, Anna Wintour has served as the editor-in-chief of Vogue magazine.

    The editor-in-chief serves as an honorary co-chair of the Gala this year alongside designer Tom Ford and Adam Mosseri.

    Wintour is a native of Hampstead, a neighborhood in London, England.

    Her career in fashion began at the age of 15, working for a boutique in London called Biba.

    Her father, Charles, was the editor of the London Evening Standard and was considered to have been “one of the greatest and most influential journalists “in the second half of the 20th century,” according to The Guardian.

  • Anna Wintour is en route

    The infamous editor-in-chief of Vogue magazine and honorary co-chair of tonight’s event left The Marks Hotel to head to the event wearing a tiara.

  • Emma Chamberlain goes platinum

    The influencer arrived to the Gala with platinum blond hair and her abdomen showing.

  • Gilded glamour, continued

    The wealthiest New Yorkers enjoyed evenings out to the opera and grand costume parties.

    Jewel tones, corsets, and plenty of fabric on dresses were all in fashion.

    Feathers were also popular, as were long gloves for ladies.

    The tuxedo made its debut in American fashion in the 1880s, which is essential to the “white tie” part of the dress code.

  • What is gilded glamour?

    Gilded glamour was the official dress code of the Met Gala for 2022.

    According to Vogue: “The 2022 Met Gala will ask its attendees to embody the grandeur—and perhaps the dichotomy—of Gilded Age New York.”

    It’s a nod to the time in which New York City truly came to life.

    “The period… was one of unprecedented prosperity, cultural change, and industrialization, when both skyscrapers and fortunes seemingly arose overnight,” the article in Vogue explains.

  • ‘Labor intensive’

    Sarah Jessica Parker said in a recent interview that the Met Gala is no regular red carpet and should be “labor intensive.”

    “Whenever I go to the Met, I don’t understand how everyone else doesn’t spend seven to 10 months working on it,” she told US Vogue.

    “The assignment is the theme, and you should interpret it. It should be labor-intensive and challenging.”

    “Like, how do you not arrive exhausted by the details of getting it right?”

  • Met Gala 2021

    After the 2020 Met Gala was canceled due to the pandemic, the Costume Institute announced a two-part version for 2021 and 2022.

    The first exhibition’s theme was In America: A Lexicon of Fashion.

    The gala, on September 13, was co-chaired by Timothée Chalamet, Billie Eilish, Amanda Gorman and Naomi Osaka.

  • Purchasing a table

    It’s also possible to purchase a table at the Gala, but this doesn’t mean you can fill it with whoever you like.

    Anyone placed at a table will need approval from Wintour.

    Some celebrities and public figures have even been banned from attending the event such as former US President Donald Trump, who tried to get his now-wife Melania Trump to attend back in 2004.

  • How do you get an invite?

    Anna Wintour, 72, Editor-in-chief at Vogue and Global Chief Content Officer at Conde Nast, has the final say in who gets to attend the event.

    Unlike other charity events, the Met Gala is invitation only and there usually is a waiting list.

    Around 500-700 guests usually attend the event, but just because you have an invite doesn’t necessarily mean you can just go.

    The event is a costume institute’s primary source of income and so those that are considered worthy enough of an invitation from Wintour will then have to pay for a seat at a table.

  • Celebrity perks

    However, many celebrities that attend this event don’t actually end up paying for their tickets and costumes for the Met Gala.

    Each celeb’s outfit is usually bankrolled by major fashion houses, in exchange for exposure and the goodwill of attending a charitable event.

  • How much are Met Gala tickets?

    Individual tickets to the Met Gala are reported to cost over $30,000, which is a steep incline since Anna Wintour took over the running of the event in 1995 when tickets were just $1,000.

    Moving on from individual tickets, there is the option of purchasing a table, which will set you back at a starting price of approximately $275,000.

  • The theme explained

    “The stories really reflect the evolution of American style, but they also explore the work of individual tailors, dress-makers, and designers,” said Andrew Bolton, head curator at the Costume Institute.

    He continued: “What’s exciting for me is that some of the names will be very familiar to students of fashion, like Charles James, Halston, and Oscar de la Renta, but a lot of the other names really have been forgotten, overlooked, or relegated into the footnotes of fashion history.

    “So one of the main intentions of the exhibition is to spotlight the talents and contributions of these individuals, and many of them are women.”

  • Blake Lively's dress

    Blake Lively made a statement by mirroring the statue of Lady Liberty in her color-changing gown.

  • Where is the Met Gala?

    Each year, the Met Gala takes place at the Metropolitan Museum of Art’s Costume Institute in New York City.

    The invite-only event highlights different fashion eras, with each theme celebrating a powerful moment in design.

    For 2022, the Met Gala’s theme is Gilded Glamour and White Tie, meant to coincide with the Costume Institute’s exhibition of In America: An Anthology of Fashion.
