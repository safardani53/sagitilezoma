Mary-Kate Olsen makes extremely rare public outing as she leaves medical building months after last

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, September 9, 2024

THE rarely-seen Mary-Kate Olsen has appeared in public after leaving a medical building.

On Wednesday, the former child star was spotted bundled up for the cold weather.

Mary-Kate, 37, wore an oversized brownish coat that extended to her black boots.

The Full House alum donned a dark blue scar that flowed past her skinny waist and denim jeans.

Oliver Sarkozy's ex-wife wore thick sunglasses.

Mary-Kate pulled her brunette hair into a messy updo bun and loosened the rest of her curly strands onto her shoulders.

It is unclear why Ashley Olsen's twin sister visited the medical building or who she met there.

IN THE CITY

The last time Mary-Kate made a public appearance was during her outing in New York over the summer. 

In June, the fashion designer enjoyed a summer day with art dealer, PC Valmorbida.

The pair had finished lunch at the Italian restaurant, Sant Ambroeus Madison, located on the Upper East Side.

The twosome were steps away from Central Park during their walk.

The New York Minute star stunned in a bright red trench coat and wore tattered jeans ripped at the ankles.

She strutted across the street in a pair of bright gold penny-loafers.

At one point, Mary-Kate dove her hand into the large brown crocodile-style bag she carried with her. 

PC had a casual look as he kept his hands inside his pockets 

He wore a light blue T-shirt under his open gray cardigan.

The art dealer paired his tanned khaki pants with Nike sneakers.

Mary-Kate's outing in the Big Apple came two years after her divorce with ex-husband Oliver, 54. 

The former couple finalized their divorce in January 2021, about nine months after their split.

The exes tied the knot in November 2015 until the ex-TV star called it quits and filed for divorce in April 2020.

Although the duo do not share any children, Oliver shares his two kids - Margot and Julien - with his ex-wife Charlotte Bernard, 49.

Mary-Kate is not the only one in her famous family who has shied away from the spotlight.

NEW MOM

Sister Ashley, 37, finally stepped out in public after she and her husband Louis Eisner, 35, welcomed their baby, Otto, in August. 

In early October, the It Takes Two actress also made the rounds in the City That Never Sleeps.

She had time away from her son while having dinner with a few of her friends in the Upper East Side.

The mom-of-one was all bundled up in a gray trench coat and an oversized knitted scarf.

She dressed in a lightly colored, loose-fitting pants and walked around in flip-flops.
