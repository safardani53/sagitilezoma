Throwing Shade at Iggy Azalea on Instagram?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Last week, Britney Spears and Iggy Azalea shot a video for their collaborative single, "Pretty Girls."

Photos from the set appeared to show Iggy and Britney getting along, but based on Ms. Spears’ recent Instagram activity, it seems the two blondes may have butted heads.

On the left is the original "Pretty Girls" cover art. On the right is a viral version in which Iggy is hilariously replaced with a bag of garbage.

Obviously, Brit didn’t have anything to do with the creation of the anti-Iggy version, but she has been busted "liking" the white trash edit.

An Instagram user named Eddyiecrz was the first (that we know of) to post the Iggy trash bag pic, and Brit was quick to give it a virtual thumbs-up.

It’s possible that Brit simply didn’t realize the pic had been edited to rag on Iggy. But let’s be real – it’s equally possible that Brit has heard Iggy freestyle rapping and realizes that she just released a single with a garbage artist.

Brit has yet to clarify things or un-like the photo, so it’s possible she still doesn’t realize she threw more shade than a mighty oak.

Of course, it’s important to point out that I-G-G-Y is not actually human garbage. These days, Iggy has so much plastic inside her she would have to be recycled.
