Kelsea Ballerini ditches her bra and underwear for daring cut-out black dress at NYC gala with boyfr

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 5, 2024

KELSEA Ballerini has flaunted her figure in new pics with her boyfriend.

The country singer was photographed with Chase Stokes, 31, at a glam event in New York City on Tuesday.

Kelsea, 30, and her partner attended the TIME100 Next event, hosted at Second Floor.

The star-studded event saw plenty of celebrity faces in attendance, such as Rachel Zegler, Maya Hawke, and Bella Ramsey.

For her look, Kelsea took on traditional with a sultry twist in a long black dress.

The Tennessee native had her hair tied back, showing off her simple yet flawless makeup.

She wore a skintight black dress with cutouts on the torso, revealing that she'd ditched her bra and panties for the night.

While the dress had long sleeves, its floor-length skirt had a thigh-high slit, showing off Kelsea's toned legs and black high heels.

At her side, Chase covered up more in a long camel coat and shiny boots.

Beneath the jacket, he color-matched Kelsea in an all-black outfit, sticking to a casual sweater and pants.

PAST LOVE

Prior to her relationship with Chase, Kelsea was married to fellow country musician Morgan Evans.

The couple divorced in 2022, after marrying in 2017, with Kelsea and Chase sparking dating rumors in January 2023.

According to Kelsea, she realized her marriage to Morgan was over due to their different family desires.

"The moment I think it was 'over over' was I realized I wasn't ready for kids, and that's a fundamental difference," the GRAMMY Award nominee recalled.

On the Call Her Daddy podcast, she told Alex Cooper: "I don't know if I want kids at all or not.

"But that was something that we had talked about early on and that was something that I was changing on."

She continued: "He was ready. He was like, 'I don't want to be an old dad,' is what he kept saying.

"I was like, 'I'm not there yet,' and I can't do that to save this [marriage] and give you something that I'm not ready for. I just can't do that to myself."

RECORD BREAKER

Born September 12, 1993, Kelsea is an American country singer and songwriter who also crosses over into pop.

She was born in Knoxville, Tennessee, and is the only child of Ed and Carla Ballerini.

In 2014, the rising star signed with Black River Entertainment and released her debut album, The First Time, the following year.

Her second album, Apologetically, came out in 2017.

To date, Kelsea has seven No. 1 singles under her belt: Love Me Like You Mean It, Legends, Dibs, Peter Pan, hole in the bottle, I Quit Drinking, and half of my hometown.

Her single, Love Me Like You Mean It, made her the first female artist to send a debut single to the top of the country music chart since Carrie Underwood in 2006.
